**Xia Dynasty Decline:** Gong Liu lost his position as agricultural official and changed to become like the Western Rong, settling at Bin.

**300+ Years Later:** Rong and Di attacked King Danfu (Grand King), who fled to the foot of Mount Qi. The people of Bin followed him and established Zhou.

**100+ Years Later:** King Wen of Zhou (Western Earl Chang) attacked the Quan Yi tribe.

**10+ Years Later:** King Wu defeated Zhou and built Luo Yi. He expelled the Rong and Yi north of the Jing and Luo rivers, establishing the 'Huangfu' (distant submission) system.

**200+ Years Later:** King Mu of Zhou attacked the Quanrong and returned with four white wolves and four white deer. After this, the Huangfu tribes stopped coming.

**200+ Years Later (771 BCE):** King You of Zhou, due to his favoritism toward Consort Baosi, had a conflict with the Marquis of Shen. The Marquis allied with Quanrong to kill King You at the foot of Mount Li. They took Zhou's Jiao-huo and settled between the Jing and Wei rivers, invading China.

**Qin Xianggong** rescued Zhou, and King Ping moved east to Luo Yi. Qin Xianggong attacked the Rong to Mount Qi and was first enfeoffed as a feudal lord.

**65 Years Later:** Shanrong crossed Yan to attack Qi, fighting Duke Xi of Qi at Qi's suburbs.

**44 Years Later:** Shanrong attacked Yan. Yan sought help from Qi. Duke Huan of Qi attacked north, and Shanrong fled.

**20+ Years Later:** Rong and Di attacked King Xiang of Zhou at Luo Yi. The King fled to Fan city in Zheng. The Rong and Di settled at Luhun, extending east to Wei, invading and plundering China.
