Yu Qing wanted to request enfeoffment for Pingyuan Lord based on Xinling Lord's preservation of Handan. Gongsun Long (公孙龙) advised against it at night, arguing: The King made Pingyuan Lord Prime Minister not for ability but because he was a relative. Accepting enfeoffment based on merit would mean calculating功劳 for a relative, which was inappropriate. Pingyuan Lord followed this advice and rejected Yu Qing's proposal. Gongsun Long was skilled at the "Hard and White" argument (坚白之辩) but was later demoted when Zou Yan visited Zhao.
