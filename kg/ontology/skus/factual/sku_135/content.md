## Key Historical Events in Lu

**Zhou Dynasty Events:**
- Year 14 of Duke Zhen: King Li fled to Zhi (彘), Gong He administered government
- Year 29 of Duke Zhen: King Xuan ascended throne
- Year 25 of Duke Xiao: Quanrong killed King You; Qin first became feudal state

**Lu Internal Conflicts:**
- Duke Yi killed by Bo Yu (usurpation reversed by King Xuan)
- Duke Yin killed by Hui at behest of Zi Yun
- Duke Huan killed in Qi by Peng Sheng (Qi compensated by executing Peng Sheng)
- Qing Fu's chaos: killed Duke Min, eventually forced to suicide

**External Relations:**
- Year 13 of Duke Zhuang: Cao Mo held Duke Huan of Qi hostage at Ke, forced return of Lu territories
- Year 15 of Duke Zhuang: Duke Huan of Qi became hegemon
- Multiple conflicts with Qi over territories
- Year 15 of Duke Cheng: First meeting with King Shoumeng of Wu
- Year 29 of Duke Xiang: Wu's Yanling Jizi visited Lu, discussed Zhou music

**Lu's Final Years:**
- Year 7 of Duke Ai: King Fuchai of Wu demanded 100 sacrificial animals from Lu
- Year 22 of Duke Ai: King Goujian of Yue destroyed Wu
- Year 24 of Duke Qing: Chu extinguished Lu, ended its sacrifices
