## Confucius (孔丘) in Lu History

**Birth:** 22nd year of Duke Xiang (襄公二十二年)

**Career in Lu:**
- Served as minister (相) during Duke Ding's meeting with Duke Jing of Qi at Jia Gu (夹谷)
- Successfully used ritual to prevent Qi from attacking Lu
- Helped negotiate return of Lu's occupied territories
- Attempted to dismantle Three Huan fortifications (year 12 of Duke Ding)
- Left Lu after Ji Huan Zi accepted Qi dancing girls

**Return:** Returned to Lu in 11th year of Duke Ai after Ji Kang Zi recalled him

**Death:** 16th year of Duke Ai (哀公十六年)

**Political Request:** Asked Duke Ai to attack Qi after Tian Chang killed Duke Jian, but was refused
