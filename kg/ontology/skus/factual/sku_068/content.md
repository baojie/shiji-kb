## Xiang Yu (项羽)

**Birth Name:** 项籍 (Xiang Ji), Courtesy Name: 羽 (Yu)
**Birthplace:** 下相
**Age at uprising:** 24 years old
**Family Lineage:**
- Uncle: 项梁
- Grandfather: 项燕, Chu general executed by Qin general Wang Jian
- Family served as Chu generals for generations, enfeoffed at 项, hence the surname Xiang

**Physical Description:** Over 8 chi tall (approximately 6 feet), strong enough to lift a bronze cauldron (力能扛鼎), exceptional talent and spirit

**Early Life:**
- Failed to complete studies in both writing and swordsmanship
- Famous quote: "Writing is only good for recording names. Swordsmanship defeats only one opponent—not worth learning. I want to learn to defeat ten thousand men."
- Studied military strategy under Xiang Liang but did not complete the study
- When Qin Shi Huang toured Kuaiji, Xiang Yu declared: "He can be replaced!" (彼可取而代也)
