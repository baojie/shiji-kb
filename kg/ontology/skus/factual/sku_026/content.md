## 伊尹

- **Name**: Also called 阿衡
- **Origin**: Served as cook/minister for 有莘氏
- **Meeting with 成汤**: Approached Tang by cooking for him and discussing the Way of Kings; alternatively, Tang sent envoys five times before Yi Yin agreed to come
- **Role**: Prime minister under 成汤; discussed 素王 and 九主 with Tang
- **Exile of 太甲**: When 太甲 proved tyrannical, Yi Yin exiled him to 桐宫 for 3 years and administered the state himself
- **Restoration**: After 太甲 reformed, Yi Yin returned power to him and wrote 太甲训三篇 praising him
- **Death**: Died during 沃丁's reign; buried at 亳
