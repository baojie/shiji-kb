## Early Qin Ancestors

### Dafei's Sons
- **Dalian** (大廉) - Bird Custom Clan (鸟俗氏)
- **Ruomu** (若木) - Fei Clan (费氏)

### Notable Descendants
- **Feichang** (费昌) - Served King Tang of Shang, defeated Jie at Mingtiao
- **Mengxi and Zhongyan** (孟戏、中衍) - Bird-bodied men who spoke human language, served Emperor Taiwu
- **Zhongyu** (中潏) - Protected the western frontier among the Western Rong
- **Feilian** (蜚廉) - Known for speed, served King Zhou of Shang
- **Elai** (恶来) - Known for strength, killed by King Wu of Zhou
- **Zaofu** (造父) - Skilled charioteer for King Mu of Zhou, granted Zhao City, ancestor of Zhao clan
- **Feizi** (非子) - Skilled horse breeder, granted Qin by King Xiao of Zhou, titled Qin Ying, continued Ying sacrifices
