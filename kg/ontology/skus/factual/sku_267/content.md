Yu Qing left Zhao because of Wei Qi (魏齐), abandoning his position worth 10,000 households. He traveled in disguise and was stranded in Liang (Wei). After Wei Qi died, Yu Qing wrote books drawing from Spring and Autumn period to recent times. He wrote 8 chapters: Jie Yi (节义), Cheng Hao (称号), Chuai Mo (揣摩), Zheng Mou (政谋), etc. The work criticized national successes and failures and was transmitted as "Yu Shi Chun Qiu" (虞氏春秋 - Mr. Yu's Spring and Autumn).
