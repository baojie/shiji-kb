## Notable Cruel Officials

### Zhi Du (郅都)
- **Origin**: Yang
- **Reputation**: Called "Blue Falcon" (苍鹰); feared by nobles and imperial clan
- **Governance**: As Governor of Jinan, exterminated the Jian clan; no theft in the commandery for over a year
- **Death**: Executed at Empress Dowager Dou's insistence after the Linjiang King incident

### Ning Cheng (宁成)
- **Origin**: Rang
- **Character**: Harsh to subordinates, bullied superiors when in lower positions
- **Wealth**: After being punished and returning home, acquired over 1,000 qing of land, controlled thousands of families

### Zhang Tang (张汤)
- **Origin**: Du
- **Childhood**: At young age, caught a rat stealing meat, conducted a full trial and execution
- **Position**: Commandant of Justice, then Censor-in-Chief
- **Method**: Would interpret laws according to emperor's wishes; praised subordinates when emperor approved, blamed them when emperor disapproved
- **Death**: Committed suicide after being accused of corruption; family wealth only 500 gold

### Yi Zong (义纵)
- **Origin**: Hedong
- **Background**: Former bandit; sister was Empress Dowager Wang's doctor
- **Governance: ** As Governor of Nanyang, destroyed Ning Cheng's family; in Dingxiang, executed over 400 people in one day
- **Death**: Executed for obstructing the告缗 (property reporting) system

### Wang Wenshu (王温舒)
- **Origin**: Yangling
- **Method**: Used local toughs as agents; gave them free rein to hunt bandits
- **Governance**: In Henei, executed over 1,000 families; blood flowed for over 10 li
- **Death**: Committed suicide when crimes exposed; five families (his and in-laws) all executed

### Du Zhou (杜周)
- **Origin**: Nanyang, Duyan
- **Method**: Outwardly lenient, inwardly harsh; followed emperor's wishes in judgments
- **Quote**: "The three-foot law comes from previous rulers' decisions; whatever the current ruler approves becomes law"
- **Wealth**: Started with one incomplete horse; ended with wealth in the tens of millions
