## 九鼎

- Ancient bronze tripods representing the nine provinces
- Symbolized legitimate rule over China
- Kept at the capital
- 楚庄王 asked about their weight during 楚庄王伐陆浑之戎 (定王元年) - implying ambition to rule
- 王孙满 responded that rule depends on virtue, not the tripods
- In 赧王59年, 秦 took the Nine Tripods when conquering 西周
- 九鼎震 occurred in 威烈王23年
