## Liu An (淮南王安)
- **Character**: Loved reading and playing zither; disliked hunting; practiced secret virtue
- **Rebellion planning**: Began after Jianyuan Year 2 (139 BCE); encouraged by Wuan Hou Tian Fen
- **Family**: Daughter Ling (intelligent, spy in Chang'an); Queen Tu; Crown Prince Qian
- **Key advisors**: Wu Bei (opposed rebellion), Zuo Wu, Zhao Xian, Zhu Jiao Ru
- **Rebellion method**: Planned to forge imperial seals, kill generals, seize strategic passes
- **Discovery**: Exposed by grandson Liu Jian's confession in Yuanshuo Year 6 (123 BCE)
- **Death**: Committed suicide before arrest; queen and crown prince executed; kingdom abolished as Jiujiang Commandery
