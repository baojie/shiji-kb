Wu kingdom possessed copper mines in Yuzhang Commandery (豫章郡铜山). Liu Bi recruited fugitives from across the empire to mint coins and boil seawater for salt. As a result, the kingdom collected no taxes from the people, yet the state treasury remained wealthy. When commoners performed corvée service, they were paid market rates. Liu Bi also provided regular rewards to talented individuals and villages. This policy continued for over 40 years.
