## Liu Bang (刘邦) - Emperor Gaozu of Han

**Birthplace:** 沛丰邑中阳里
**Surname:** 刘, Courtesy Name: 季
**Father:** 太公, Mother: 刘媪

**Physical Description:** High nose bridge (隆准), dragon-like countenance (龙颜), beautiful beard, 72 black moles on left thigh

**Character:** Benevolent and loved people, generous, broad-minded, did not engage in family productive work

**Early Career:**
- Served as 亭长 of Sishui
- Known for loving wine and women
- Once saw Qin Shi Huang on tour and sighed: "A great man should be like this!" (大丈夫当如此也)

**Marriage:** Married 吕公's daughter (later Empress Lü), who bore Emperor Hui and Princess Lu Yuan

**Legend of the White Snake:**
- While drunk, cut a large snake blocking his path
- An old woman claimed the snake was the White Emperor's son, killed by the Red Emperor's son (Liu Bang)
- This became part of his divine legitimacy

**Establishment of Han:**
- Became King of Han in 206 BCE
- Proclaimed Emperor in 202 BCE
- Capital: Initially Luoyang, later Chang'an
