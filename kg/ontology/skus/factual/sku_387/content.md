Modu (冒顿) was the crown prince of Chanyu Touman (头曼). Touman wanted to replace Modu with a younger son from a favored consort, so he sent Modu as a hostage to the Yuezhi and then attacked them, hoping they would kill Modu. Modu stole a fine horse and escaped back to Xiongnu territory.

Touman, impressed by his bravery, gave Modu command of 10,000 cavalry. Modu created whistling arrows (鸣镝) and trained his men: 'Anyone who does not shoot at whatever my whistling arrow shoots at will be executed.'

**Training progression:**
1. First, he shot the whistling arrow at birds and beasts during hunting; those who didn't shoot were executed.
2. Then he shot his own fine horse; those who hesitated were executed.
3. Then he shot his beloved wife; those who hesitated were executed.
4. Finally, during a hunt with his father, he shot the whistling arrow at Touman's fine horse, and all his men shot at it.

Modu then shot the whistling arrow at his father Touman during a hunt; all his followers shot and killed the Chanyu. Modu executed his stepmothers, younger brothers, and any officials who wouldn't obey him, then declared himself Chanyu.
