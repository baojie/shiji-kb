**Qin Empire Boundaries (after unification)**
- **East**: To the sea and Korea
- **West**: To Lintao and Qiang territory
- **South**: To Beixianghu (northern door)
- **North**: Along Yellow River as defense line, including Yin Mountains to Liaodong

**Major Construction Projects**
- Epang Palace (阿房宫): Front hall 500 paces east-west, 50 zhang north-south, could seat 10,000 people
- Lishan Mausoleum: 700,000+ laborers, contained rivers of mercury, astronomical ceiling, geographical floor
- Great Wall: Built by General Meng Tian with 300,000 troops against Xiongnu
- Lingqu Canal and various roads including imperial highways (驰道)
