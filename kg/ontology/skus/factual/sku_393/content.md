**Qin:**
- King Hui took 25 cities from Yiqu
- King Zhao's mother (Queen Dowager Xuan) had an affair with the Yiqu Rong King and bore two sons, then killed him at Ganquan and destroyed Yiqu
- Qin acquired Longxi, Beidi, and Shangjun commanderies
- Built the Great Wall to resist the Hu

**Zhao:**
- King Wuling adopted Hu clothing and trained cavalry archery
- Defeated Linhu and Loufan to the north
- Built Great Wall from Dai along Mount Yin to Gaque
- Established Yunzhong, Yanmen, and Dai commanderies

**Yan:**
- General Qin Kai, who had been a hostage among the Hu and gained their trust, returned and launched a surprise attack, driving Donghu back over 1,000 li
- Built Great Wall from Zaoyang to Xiangping
- Established Shanggu, Yuyang, You Beiping, Liaoxi, and Liaodong commanderies

**Qin Dynasty:**
- General Meng Tian led 100,000 troops north against the Hu, recovering the Henan region
- Built 44 walled towns along the Yellow River
- Constructed the Straight Road from Jiuyuan to Yunyang
- Built fortifications from Lintao to Liaodong, over 10,000 li
