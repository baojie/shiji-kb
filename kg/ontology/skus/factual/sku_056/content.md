**Imperial Tours and Inscriptions**

**Year 27 (220 BCE)**: Tour of Longxi and Beidi, via Jitou Mountain and Huizhong

**Year 28 (219 BCE)**: Eastern tour
- Climbed Zouyi Mountain, erected stele
- Climbed Mount Tai, performed Feng and Shan sacrifices
- Visited Langya, stayed 3 months, erected Langya Terrace stele
- Sent Xu Fu with thousands of boys and girls to seek immortals

**Year 29 (218 BCE)**: Eastern tour
- Attacked by assassin at Bolangsha, Yangwu
- Climbed Zhifu, erected stele

**Year 32 (215 BCE)**: Tour to Jieshi
- Sent Lu Sheng to seek immortals
- Ordered Meng Tian to attack Xiongnu with 300,000 troops

**Year 37 (210 BCE)**: Final tour
- Visited Yunmeng, worshipped Yu Shun at Jiuyi Mountain
- Traveled via Yangtze to Qiantang, climbed Kuaiji Mountain
- Worshipped Yu the Great, erected stele
- Died at Shaqiu on return journey
