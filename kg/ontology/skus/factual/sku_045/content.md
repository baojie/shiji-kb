## Qin Territorial Expansion

### Early Period
- **Duke Xiang**: Granted lands west of Qi (岐以西) by King Ping of Zhou
- **Duke Wen**: Recovered Zhou's remaining people, territory reached Qi Mountain

### Duke Mu Period
- **Year 37**: Defeated Rong King, added 12 states, opened 1000 li of territory, became Hegemon of Western Rong
- Territory reached the Yellow River in the east

### King Hui Wen Period
- **Year 8**: Wei ceded Hexi (河西) region
- **Year 9**: Took Fenyin (汾阴), Pishi (皮氏)
- **Year 10**: Wei ceded Shang Commandery (上郡) with 15 counties
- **Year 13**: Took 600 li of Hanzhong from Chu, established Hanzhong Commandery

### King Zhao Xiang Period
- **Year 21**: Wei ceded Anyi (安邑)
- **Year 27**: Took Qianzhong (黔中) from Chu
- **Year 29**: Took Chu capital Ying, established Nan Commandery (南郡)
- **Year 30**: Took Wu Commandery (巫郡) and Jiangnan as Qianzhong Commandery
- **Year 33**: Took Nanyang (南阳) from Wei
- **Year 47**: Took Shangdang (上党) from Han
- **Year 51**: Took 36 cities from Western Zhou
- **Year 52**: Acquired Nine Ding (九鼎), Zhou effectively ended

### King Zhuang Xiang Period
- **Year 1**: Took Chenggao (成皋) and Gong (巩) from Han, established Sanchuan Commandery (三川郡)
- **Year 3**: Took 37 cities from Zhao, established Taiyuan Commandery (太原郡)
