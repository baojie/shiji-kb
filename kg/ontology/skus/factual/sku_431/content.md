## Chunyu Kun (淳于髡)
- **Origin**: Qi; son-in-law who married into wife's family
- **Appearance**: Under 7 chi tall
- **Famous advice**: Told King Wei of Qi about a bird that "doesn't fly for three years, but when it flies, it reaches the sky" - prompting the king to reform
- **Diplomacy**: Obtained 100,000 Zhao troops to repel Chu invasion by mocking the king's meager gifts
- **Drinking capacity**: Could get drunk on 1 dou or 1 shi depending on circumstances; used this to advise against excessive drinking

## You Meng (优孟)
- **Origin**: Chu
- **Position**: Court musician
- **Famous deed**: When King Zhuang wanted to bury his beloved horse with minister's rites, You Meng suggested emperor's rites to show the absurdity
- **Helping Sun Shu'ao's son**: Impersonated the deceased minister so convincingly that the king offered him the position; then sang about how minister's sons end up poor, prompting the king to grant Sun's son 400 households

## You Zhan (优旃)
- **Origin**: Qin
- **Appearance**: Dwarf
- **Kindness**: Got relief for guards standing in rain by joking about his shortness
- **Preventing waste**: Stopped Qin Shi Huang's plan for huge hunting park by saying it would be convenient for enemies to invade
- **Stopping lacquer project**: Stopped Qin Er Shi's plan to lacquer the city walls by joking about the difficulty of finding a large enough drying room
