**Qin Shi Huang (秦始皇)**
- **Birth**: Born in the 48th year of King Zhao of Qin (259 BCE), in Handan, Zhao
- **Father**: King Zhuangxiang of Qin (秦庄襄王), who was a hostage prince in Zhao
- **Mother**: A concubine of Lü Buwei whom Zhuangxiang took as wife
- **Birth Name**: Zheng (政), surname Zhao (赵氏)
- **Accession**: Became King of Qin at age 13 when Zhuangxiang died (246 BCE)
- **Early Reign**: State affairs were entrusted to ministers due to his young age
