## Zheng Dang Shi (郑当时)
- **Origin**: Chen; descendant of a general under Xiang Yu
- **Character**: Generous, loved knights and scholars; studied Huang-Lao philosophy
- **Hospitality**: Every five days on leave, would host guests continuously; feared not seeing everyone
- **Positions**: Crown Prince's attendant, Governor of Lu, Governor of Jinan, Chancellor of Jiangdu, Right Internal Historian, Minister of Agriculture
- **Poverty**: Despite high positions, died poor; family had no surplus wealth
- **Descendants**: Brothers and grandsons reached 2,000-picul rank six or seven times
