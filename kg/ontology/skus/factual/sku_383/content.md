**Top Officials (24 Great Chiefs called 'Ten Thousand Horsemen'):**
- Left and Right Wise Kings (贤王)
- Left and Right Guli Kings (谷蠡王)
- Left and Right Great Generals (大将)
- Left and Right Great Commandants (大都尉)
- Left and Right Great Household Officials (大当户)
- Left and Right Gudu Marquises (骨都侯)

**Note:** The Xiongnu called 'wise' as 'Tuqi' (屠耆), so the crown prince was usually made Left Tuqi King.

**Noble Clans:** The Huyan (呼衍氏), Lan (兰氏), and later Xubo (须卜氏) clans were the noble lineages.

**Subordinate Officials:** Each of the 24 great chiefs appointed their own thousand-commanders, hundred-commanders, ten-commanders, minor kings, ministers, commandants, household officials, and other functionaries.
