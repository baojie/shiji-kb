## Zi Chan (子产)
- **Position**: Prime Minister of Zheng during Duke Zhao's reign
- **Achievements**:
  - Year 1: Children stopped playing pranks; elderly not burdened with carrying loads
  - Year 2: Markets had fair prices
  - Year 3: Gates not locked at night; lost items not taken
  - Year 4: Farm tools not left unattended
  - Year 5: No military conscription needed; mourning observed without orders
- **Death**: Died after 26 years of governance; people mourned greatly
