## Wei's Territorial Losses to Qin

**Under King Xiang:**
- Year 5: Lost 45,000 troops at Diaoyin
- Year 5: Ceded Hexi region
- Year 6: Lost Fenyin, Pishi, Jiao
- Year 7: Ceded entire Shang Commandery
- Year 8: Lost Quwo, Pingzhou

**Under King Ai:**
- Year 5: Lost Quwo
- Year 16: Lost Pufan, Yangjin, Fengling

**Under King Zhao:**
- Year 1: Lost Xiangcheng
- Year 6: Ceded 400 li of Hedong
- Year 7: Lost 61 cities (large and small)
- Year 9: Lost Xinyuan, Quyang
- Year 13: Lost Ancheng

**Under King Anxi:**
- Year 1: Lost 2 cities
- Year 2: Lost 2 more cities
- Year 3: Lost 4 cities, 40,000 killed
- Year 4: 150,000 killed
- Year 9: Lost Huai
- Year 11: Lost Siqiu

**Under King Jingmin:**
- Year 1: Lost 20 cities (became Qin's Dong Commandery)
- Year 2: Lost Chaoge
- Year 3: Lost Ji
- Year 5: Lost Yuan, Puyang, Yan
