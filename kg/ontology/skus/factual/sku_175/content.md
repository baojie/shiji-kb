After seven years of rebuilding, Goujian planned revenge. Minister Feng Tong advised forming alliances with Qi, Chu, and Jin while waiting for Wu to overextend. When Wu attacked Qi and defeated them at Ailing (艾陵), Wu Zixu warned that 'Yue is the heart ailment of Wu; Qi is merely a skin disease.' King Fuchai ignored this and eventually forced Wu Zixu to commit suicide with the Shulou sword (属镂剑). Wu Zixu's last words: 'Place my eyes on Wu's east gate to watch the Yue army enter!' In 482 BCE, while Fuchai was at the Huangchi (黄池) meeting with feudal lords, Yue attacked with 2,000 naval troops, 40,000 trained soldiers, 6,000 elite guards, and 1,000 officers, killing the Wu crown prince. After four more years, Yue besieged Wu for three years until Fuchai was trapped on Gusu Mountain. Fuchai requested surrender, but Fan Li refused, citing 'Heaven gave Wu to Yue.' Fuchai committed suicide, covering his face saying 'I have no face to meet Wu Zixu!' Goujian then executed Bo Pi.
