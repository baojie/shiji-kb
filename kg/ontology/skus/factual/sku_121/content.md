## Founding of Qi State

### Enfeoffment
- **Location**: Yingqiu (营丘) in Qi
- **Time**: After King Wu conquered Shang and became ruler of the world

### Early Challenges
- Lai people (莱人, Yi tribe) attacked to contest Yingqiu
- Lai took advantage of Zhou's recent establishment and inability to control distant regions
- Taigong Wang traveled through the night to arrive in time

### Governance Policies
- Adapted to local customs (因其俗)
- Simplified rituals (简其礼)
- Developed commerce and industry (通商工之业)
- Exploited fishing and salt resources (便鱼盐之利)
- Result: People flocked to Qi, making it a major state

### Military Authority
During Duke Cheng of Zhou's reign, when Guan and Cai rebelled and Huai Yi betrayed Zhou:
- Duke Kang of Shao was sent to grant Taigong Wang authority:
  - **East**: To the sea
  - **West**: To the Yellow River
  - **South**: To Muling (穆陵)
  - **North**: To Wudi (无棣)
- Authority to campaign against "five lords and nine earls" (五侯九伯)
- This made Qi a major military power

### Capital
- Yingqiu (营丘)
