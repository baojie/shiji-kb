**Wei Qing (卫青)** - Grand General of Han Dynasty

- **Origin**: Pingyang (平阳人)
- **Father**: Zheng Ji (郑季), a clerk who served at the Marquis of Pingyang's household
- **Mother**: Wei Ao (卫媪), a concubine of the Marquis of Pingyang
- **Birth**: Born from affair between Zheng Ji and Wei Ao
- **Siblings**: Wei Changzi (卫长子, older brother), Wei Ru (卫孺, eldest sister), Shao'er (少儿, second sister), Wei Zifu (卫子夫, third sister who became Empress), Bu (步) and Guang (广, younger brothers)
- **Courtesy name**: Zhongqing (仲卿)
- **Early life**: As a youth, herded sheep for his father; half-brothers treated him as a slave
- **Prediction**: A prisoner at Ganquan predicted he would become a marquis
- **Posthumous title**: Lie Hou (烈侯)
