## Duke Huan of Qi (齐桓公)

### Background
- **Personal name**: Xiao Bai (小白)
- **Father**: Duke Li of Qi
- **Mother**: From Wei (卫女), favored by Duke Xi
- **Exile**: Fled to Ju (莒) during Duke Xiang's reign due to fear of disaster
- **Tutor**: Bao Shuya (鲍叔牙)

### Competition for Throne
After Gongsun Wuzhi killed Duke Xiang and was himself killed by people of Yonglin:
- **Prince Jiu** (纠): Fled to Lu; mother was from Lu; tutors were Guan Zhong and Shao Hu
- **Prince Xiao Bai**: Fled to Ju; had support from Gao Xi and Guo families

### Race for Capital
- Lu sent troops to escort Prince Jiu
- Guan Zhong led troops to intercept Xiao Bai's route from Ju
- Guan Zhong shot Xiao Bai, hitting his belt hook (带钩)
- Xiao Bai feigned death, causing Lu to delay
- Xiao Bai arrived first, was enthroned as Duke Huan

### Appointment of Guan Zhong
- Duke Huan initially wanted to kill Guan Zhong for revenge
- **Bao Shuya's recommendation**: "If you want to rule Qi, Gao Xi and I are sufficient. If you want to become hegemon, you need Guan Yiwu."
- Guan Zhong was released from bonds at Tangfu, purified and presented to Duke Huan
- Appointed as grand officer and given charge of government

### Key Advisors
- Guan Zhong (管仲)
- Bao Shuya (鲍叔牙)
- Xi Peng (隰朋)
- Gao Xi (高傒)
