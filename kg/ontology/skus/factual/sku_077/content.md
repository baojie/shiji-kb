## Emperor Wu of Han (孝武帝)

**Personal Name:** 刘彻
**Mother:** Empress Wang
**Reign:** 141-87 BCE

**Religious and Supernatural Pursuits:**

**Fangshi (方士) and Immortals:**
- 李少君: Claimed to be over 700 years old, taught stove worship and immortality methods
- 少翁: Created "Wen Cheng General," summoned spirits of deceased Consort Wang
- 栾大: Granted five gold seals simultaneously, married Emperor's daughter, later executed for fraud
- 公孙卿: Claimed to have received texts about Yellow Emperor's immortality

**Fengshan Sacrifices (封禅):**
- Performed at Mount Tai in 110 BCE
- First emperor since Qin Shi Huang to perform this ritual
- Established the "August One" (泰一) as supreme deity
- Built Mingtang (明堂) at Mount Tai

**New Era Names (年号):**
- 建元: First era name in Chinese history
- 元光: Named after a comet
- 元狩: Named after capturing a unicorn-like beast
- 元鼎: Named after discovery of a sacred tripod
- 元封: Named after fengshan sacrifice
- 太初: Calendar reform, adopted first month as year start

**Major Construction:**
- Jianzhang Palace (建章宫): 1,000 gates, 10,000 rooms
- Tongtian Terrace (通天台): Over 50 zhang high
- Weiyang Palace expanded

**Military Campaigns:**
- Conquered Nanyue (南越)
- Campaigns against Xiongnu
- Expedition to Dayuan (大宛)

**Calendar Reform:** Changed to Taichu calendar, first month as year beginning, yellow as imperial color
