## Chu-Qin Military Conflicts

### Zhang Yi's Deception
- Zhang Yi (张仪) of Qin pretended to leave Qin and offered to serve Chu
- Promised 600 li of Shang-Yu land if Chu broke relations with Qi
- King Huai believed him and broke with Qi
- Zhang Yi then claimed only 6 li was promised

### Battle of Dan and Xi (丹、淅)
- King Huai attacked Qin in anger
- Qin defeated Chu at Dan and Xi
- **Casualties**: 80,000 Chu soldiers killed
- **Prisoner**: Chu general Qu Gai (屈匄) captured
- **Territory lost**: Han Zhong region taken by Qin

### Battle of Lantian (蓝田)
- King Huai sent all national forces deep into Qin
- Wei attacked Chu from behind at Deng
- Chu forces retreated in fear
- Qi refused to help Chu due to anger
- Chu was greatly weakened

### King Huai's Capture
- King Zhao of Qin invited King Huai for a meeting
- Qu Yuan advised against going, calling Qin a "tiger and wolf state"
- Prince Zilan urged the king to go
- King Huai entered Wu Pass; Qin forces cut off his retreat
- King held hostage for territory demands
- King refused, fled to Zhao (was not accepted), returned to Qin
- **Death**: Died in Qin, body returned for burial
