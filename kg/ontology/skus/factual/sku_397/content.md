Li Ling led 5,000 infantry 1,000+ li north of Juyan to engage the Chanyu. He killed over 10,000 enemies but ran out of supplies and was surrounded. He surrendered to the Xiongnu.

Only 400 of his men returned to Han. The Chanyu valued Li Ling and gave him his daughter in marriage.

**Li Ling's Background:** Grandson of the famous general Li Guang.
