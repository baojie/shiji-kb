## Lord Xinling (信陵君无忌)

**Background:** Son of King Zhao of Wei, brother of King Anxi

**Major Deeds:**

1. **Saving Zhao (Year 20 of King Anxi):**
   - Qin besieged Handan (Zhao's capital)
   - Lord Xinling forged orders and seized General Jin Bi's army
   - Used the army to save Zhao
   - Remained in Zhao afterward

2. **Victory over Qin (Year 30 of King Anxi):**
   - Returned to Wei
   - Led armies of five states against Qin
   - Defeated Qin at Hewai
   - Put Meng Ao to flight

**Death:** Died in Year 34 of King Anxi

**Legacy:** The text suggests Wei's decline was attributed by some to not employing Lord Xinling properly, though the Grand Historian offers a different view that Heaven destined Qin to unify China.
