**Modu Chanyu (冒顿单于):** Killed his father Touman and seized power. Made Xiongnu most powerful. Reigned 1000+ years from Chunwei to Touman (size fluctuated, often divided).

**Laoshang Chanyu (老上单于):** Son of Modu, named Jiyu (稽粥). Continued conflicts with Han. Zhonghang Yue served him.

**Junchen Chanyu (军臣单于):** Son of Laoshang. Broke Heqin and invaded Shangjun and Yunzhong with 30,000 cavalry each. Died during Han campaigns.

**Yizhixie Chanyu (伊稚斜单于):** Brother of Junchen, Left Guli King. Seized power after defeating Junchen's crown prince Yudan (who fled to Han and was made She'an Marquis but died months later). Reigned 13 years.

**Uwei Chanyu (乌维单于):** Son of Yizhixie. Reigned 10 years (Yuanding era). Han focused on conquering Nanyue.

**Ushilu/'Child' Chanyu (兒单于):** Son of Uwei, named Ushilu. Young, reigned 3 years. Died of illness while planning to attack Shouxiang City.

**Goulihu Chanyu (呴犁湖单于):** Younger brother of Uwei, Right Wise King. Reigned 1 year. Died of illness.

**Andinghou Chanyu (且鞮侯单于):** Younger brother of Uwei, Left Great Commander. Released Han envoys. Called himself 'son' of Han emperor.
