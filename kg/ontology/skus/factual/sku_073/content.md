## Establishment of Han Dynasty

**Three Articles of Law (约法三章):**
When Liu Bang entered Xianyang, he proclaimed:
1. Murderers shall be put to death
2. Those who injure others shall be punished
3. Thieves shall pay for their crimes
All other Qin laws were abolished

**Division of the Empire (206 BCE):**
Xiang Yu divided the empire among 18 kings:
- Liu Bang: King of Han (汉王), ruling Ba, Shu, and Hanzhong, capital Nanzheng
- 章邯 (Zhang Han): King of Yong (雍王), capital Feiqiu
- 司马欣 (Sima Xin): King of Sai (塞王), capital Yueyang
- 董翳 (Dong Yi): King of Zhai (翟王), capital Gaonu
- Xiang Yu: Hegemon-King of Western Chu (西楚霸王), 9 commanderies, capital Pengcheng

**Liu Bang's Three Heroes (汉初三杰):**
Liu Bang stated: "In planning strategies within the tent and determining victory a thousand li away, I am not equal to Zhang Liang. In governing the state, comforting the people, and ensuring supplies, I am not equal to Xiao He. In commanding millions of troops, winning every battle and taking every city, I am not equal to Han Xin. These three are heroes, and my ability to use them is why I gained the world."

**Imperial Proclamation:** Liu Bang became Emperor in 202 BCE at 汜水之阳
