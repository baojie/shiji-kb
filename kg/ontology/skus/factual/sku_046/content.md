## Qin Surname Branches

According to Sima Qian, the Qin ancestors bore the **Ying surname** (嬴姓). Their descendants were enfeoffed in various states and took their state names as surnames:

- **Xu clan** (徐氏)
- **Tan clan** (郯氏)
- **Ju clan** (莒氏)
- **Zhongli clan** (终黎氏)
- **Yunyan clan** (运奄氏)
- **Tuqiu clan** (菟裘氏)
- **Jiangliang clan** (将梁氏)
- **Huang clan** (黄氏)
- **Jiang clan** (江氏)
- **Xiuyu clan** (脩鱼氏)
- **Baiming clan** (白冥氏)
- **Feilian clan** (蜚廉氏)
- **Qin clan** (秦氏)

Additionally, because the ancestor **Zaofu** was granted Zhao City (赵城), some branches became the **Zhao clan** (赵氏). This is why Qin rulers were also referred to with the Zhao surname.
