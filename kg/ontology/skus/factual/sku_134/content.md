## State of Lu (鲁国)

**Founder:** Duke of Zhou (周公旦), enfeoffed at Qufu (曲阜)

**Location:** Former capital of Shaohao (少昊之虚)

**Total Rulers:** 34 generations from Duke of Zhou to Duke Qing

**End:** Extinguished by Chu Kaolie Wang (楚考烈王) in 24th year of Duke Qing

**Special Privileges:** Granted right to perform suburban sacrifices to King Wen and use royal ritual music (to honor Duke of Zhou's virtue)

**Governance Style:** Bo Qin took 3 years to report governance because he changed customs and reformed rituals; contrasted with Taigong of Qi who simplified rituals and followed customs (5 months to report)

**Famous Prediction:** Duke of Zhou predicted that Lu would eventually serve Qi in the north due to Lu's complex governance vs Qi's simplicity
