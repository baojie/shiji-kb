# Govern Through Accessible Justice

## Overview
A leadership approach where rulers personally tour their territory and administer justice in accessible, public settings, ensuring all people from nobles to commoners receive fair treatment.

## Steps

1. **Tour your territory regularly**
   - Visit villages and towns in person
   - Be physically present among the people

2. **Establish accessible venues for administration**
   - Choose simple, public locations (e.g., under a tree)
   - Avoid barriers between ruler and people

3. **Handle legal cases and government affairs on-site**
   - Make decisions in the open
   - Resolve disputes promptly

4. **Ensure fair outcomes for all social levels**
   - From nobility to commoners, each person should receive appropriate treatment
   - No one should lose their proper role or position due to negligence

## Expected Outcomes
- People develop deep trust and affection for leadership
- Harmony between ruler and subjects
- Legacy of good governance that people remember and honor
