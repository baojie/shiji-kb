# Military Oath and Mobilization Procedure

## Overview
A method for military commanders to mobilize troops, establish authority, and ensure discipline through ritualized oath-taking and clear consequences.

## Steps

1. **Display Symbols of Authority**
   - Hold the yellow battle-axe (黄钺) in the left hand
   - Hold the white banner (白旄) in the right hand
   - These symbols represent delegated military authority from the ruler

2. **Deliver the Oath**
   - Address the troops with a clear call (e.g., "苍兕苍兕")
   - Gather all personnel and equipment
   - State the mission objectives clearly

3. **Establish Consequences**
   - Set clear deadlines for assembly or action
   - Announce penalties for non-compliance (e.g., "後至者斩" - those who are late will be executed)
   - Ensure all understand the stakes

4. **Execute the Campaign**
   - March to the designated location
   - Maintain formation and discipline

## Decision Points
- Before engaging enemy forces, assess whether conditions favor victory
- If allies are not fully committed, consider delaying engagement
- Evaluate whether the political timing is right for decisive action

## Expected Outcomes
- Unified military force with clear chain of command
- Troops understand expectations and consequences
- Enhanced morale through ritual and clarity
