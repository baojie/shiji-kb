# Credibility Establishment Method (from Shang Yang's governance)

## Overview
A technique for building public trust before implementing major policy changes.

## Steps

### 1. Create a Simple, Verifiable Task
- Place a wooden pole (three zhang tall) at the city's south gate
- Announce: "Whoever moves this pole to the north gate will receive 10 gold pieces"

### 2. Observe Public Reaction
- People will be suspicious and hesitant
- No one will act immediately

### 3. Increase the Stakes
- Raise the reward significantly (to 50 gold)
- This signals seriousness and commitment

### 4. Reward the First Actor
- When someone finally performs the task, immediately pay the full reward
- This demonstrates that promises will be kept

### 5. Implement Your Actual Policies
- Now that credibility is established, announce the real laws and reforms
- People will believe and comply

## Key Principle
"疑行无名，疑事无功" - Those who doubt their actions gain no reputation; those who doubt their undertakings achieve no success.

## Expected Outcome
Public trust in government announcements, enabling smooth implementation of reforms.
