# Five Criteria for Official Evaluation

This is an ancient Chinese framework attributed to Li Ke for comprehensively evaluating candidates for government positions.

## Overview
Evaluate a person's character through five distinct lenses to determine their suitability for office.

## The Five Criteria

1. **居视其所亲** - Observe who they associate with in daily life
   - Their choice of companions reveals their values
   - Look at their close friends and associates

2. **富视其所与** - Observe how they distribute wealth when prosperous
   - Do they share generously or hoard?
   - What causes do they support financially?

3. **达视其所举** - Observe whom they recommend when in power
   - Do they promote capable people or favorites?
   - Quality of their recommendations reflects judgment

4. **穷视其所不为** - Observe what they refuse to do when facing difficulties
   - What principles do they maintain under pressure?
   - What lines will they not cross?

5. **贫视其所不取** - Observe what they refuse to accept when poor
   - Do they maintain integrity despite poverty?
   - What bribes or ill-gotten gains do they reject?

## Expected Outcome
These five criteria provide sufficient information to determine a candidate's true character and suitability for office without needing additional investigation.
