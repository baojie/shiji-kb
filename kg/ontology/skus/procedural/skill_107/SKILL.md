# Endurance and Reminder Practice

## Overview
A self-discipline procedure for maintaining focus on long-term goals and remembering past humiliation, based on Goujian's "tasting gall" practice.

## Steps

1. **Create physical reminder** - Place a bitter object (gall bladder) in living space
2. **Establish ritual** - Look at or taste the reminder when sitting, lying down, and eating
3. **Verbal affirmation** - Ask yourself: "Have you forgotten the shame of [your defeat]?"
4. **Practice austerity** - Personally farm, have spouse weave, eat simple food, wear plain clothes
5. **Humble yourself** - Treat worthy people with respect, be generous to guests
6. **Share hardships** - Work alongside common people to build loyalty

## Core Principle
Constant physical and behavioral reminders maintain focus on long-term goals during recovery.

## Expected Outcomes
- Sustained motivation over years
- Building of internal strength and popular support
- Readiness for revenge when opportunity arises
