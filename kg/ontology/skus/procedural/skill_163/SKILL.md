# Intelligence Network Espionage

## Overview
Deploy guests and associates to infiltrate rival organizations and report on leadership decisions and secret plans.

## Steps
1. Identify key targets whose decisions impact your strategic interests
2. Place trusted agents within their sphere of influence
3. Establish secure communication channels for agents to report intelligence
4. Verify intelligence through multiple sources when possible
5. Use gathered intelligence to anticipate actions and prepare responses

## Decision Points
- Balance intelligence gathering against risk of exposure
- Determine which intelligence warrants action vs. monitoring

## Expected Outcomes
- Advance warning of threats or opportunities
- Strategic advantage through superior information
- Enhanced reputation for wisdom when predictions prove accurate
