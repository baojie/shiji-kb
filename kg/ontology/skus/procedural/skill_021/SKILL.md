# Undermine Enemy Advisor

## Overview
A counterintelligence procedure to neutralize talented advisors serving rival states by creating distrust between them and their rulers. Based on Qin's handling of You Yu serving the Rong king.

## Steps

1. **Recognize the Threat**
   - Identify capable advisors in rival states
   - Acknowledge that talented enemies are dangerous

2. **Assess the Advisor's Value**
   - Engage the advisor in conversation
   - Learn about their knowledge of geography and military affairs
   - Understand their relationship with their ruler

3. **Deploy Distraction Tactics**
   - Send entertainment (music, performers) to the rival ruler
   - Aim to occupy the ruler's attention and reduce governance focus

4. **Create Suspicion**
   - Request the advisor stay longer than planned
   - Make the rival ruler question the advisor's loyalty
   - Create apparent closeness between yourself and the advisor

5. **Exploit the Breach**
   - When suspicion takes hold, the advisor may be dismissed or flee
   - Offer refuge if the advisor seeks to defect

6. **Integrate the Defector**
   - Treat the defector with honor as a guest
   - Acquire their strategic knowledge of the former state

## Decision Points
- If the rival ruler is isolated and unfamiliar with your culture, they are more vulnerable
- If the advisor is already frustrated with their ruler, defection is more likely

## Expected Outcomes
- Removal of capable enemy advisor
- Potential gain of strategic intelligence
- Weakened rival state governance
