# Night Attack on Encamped Enemy

## Overview
Tactical procedure for conducting a surprise night assault against an enemy encampment, based on Wei Qing's attack on the Xiongnu Right Wise King.

## Preconditions
- Enemy believes they are beyond reach of your forces
- Enemy has become complacent (e.g., drunk)
- Your forces can approach undetected

## Steps

1. **Approach Undetected**
   - Force march to reach enemy position
   - Arrive at night when enemy least expects

2. **Encircle the Camp**
   - Surround the enemy position under cover of darkness
   - Cut off escape routes

3. **Launch Surprise Attack**
   - Attack while enemy is unprepared
   - Enemy leadership will panic and attempt to flee

4. **Pursue Fleeing Leadership**
   - Enemy commander will escape with small escort
   - Dispatch light cavalry to pursue (hundreds of li)

5. **Capture Secondary Targets**
   - If primary target escapes, capture subordinate kings and nobles
   - Seize population, livestock, and supplies

6. **Withdraw with Captures**
   - Return to friendly territory with prisoners and booty

## Expected Outcome
Even if the primary enemy leader escapes, capture of subordinate leaders, soldiers, civilians, and livestock constitutes a major victory.
