# Military Deception Tactics (from Sun Bin's strategies)

## Overview
Techniques for deceiving enemies by manipulating their perceptions of your strength and intentions.

## Tactic 1: False Weakness Through Staged Retreat

### Steps
1. Enter enemy territory and establish initial presence
2. Gradually reduce visible signs of your forces (e.g., cooking fires)
3. Pattern: Day 1 = 100,000 fires → Day 2 = 50,000 fires → Day 3 = 30,000 fires
4. Enemy interprets this as mass desertion and becomes overconfident
5. Enemy rushes forward with light forces, abandoning heavy support
6. Set ambush at narrow, defensible position
7. Strike when enemy is committed and vulnerable

## Tactic 2: Asymmetric Resource Allocation

### Steps
1. Assess your resources against opponent's (divide into tiers: upper, middle, lower)
2. Sacrifice your weakest against their strongest (accept one loss)
3. Deploy your strongest against their middle tier (secure one win)
4. Deploy your middle against their weakest (secure second win)
5. Result: 2 wins, 1 loss = overall victory

## Expected Outcome
Victory against numerically superior forces through strategic deception and resource optimization.
