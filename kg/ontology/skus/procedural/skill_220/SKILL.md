# Accepting Mass Enemy Surrender

## Overview
Procedure for safely accepting the surrender of large enemy forces (tens of thousands), based on Huo Qubing's handling of the Xiongnu Hunye King's surrender.

## Steps

1. **Receive Initial Contact**
   - Enemy sends envoys to border requesting surrender
   - Border commander relays message to central authority

2. **Assess Credibility**
   - Sovereign considers possibility of feigned surrender to launch surprise attack
   - Dispatch a trusted general with military force to receive the surrender

3. **Approach with Military Force**
   - Cross the border with armed troops
   - Make visual contact with surrendering force

4. **Identify and Neutralize Dissenters**
   - Some in the surrendering force may try to flee or resist
   - Enter the enemy camp with elite cavalry
   - Execute those attempting to escape (e.g., 8,000 executed)

5. **Secure the Leadership**
   - Escort the enemy leader separately to the capital
   - Use official transport (传) for the leader

6. **Process the Mass of Surrenderers**
   - Transport the surrendered population across the border
   - Provide food and supplies during transit

7. **Resettle the Population**
   - Distribute surrendered people across border regions
   - Allow them to maintain their customs
   - Establish as subordinate territories (属国)

8. **Reward the Leadership**
   - Confer titles and fiefs on surrendered leaders
   - Provide generous gifts to ensure loyalty

## Expected Outcome
Successful integration of surrendered forces, reduced border threats, and expanded territory without major conflict.
