# Form Alliance Coalition

## Overview
A diplomatic strategy for creating alliances among multiple parties to counter a dominant threat through coordinated action.

## Steps

1. **Identify shared threat**
   - Recognize when a powerful actor endangers multiple parties
   - Confirm that others perceive the same threat

2. **Send envoys with resources**
   - Provide gifts (horses, gold, silk) to demonstrate commitment
   - Equip representatives to negotiate effectively

3. **Secure commitment from key partners**
   - Target influential parties first
   - Gain their endorsement to attract others

4. **Establish coordination structure**
   - Designate leadership for the alliance
   - Define mutual obligations and contributions

5. **Strengthen ties through marriage or exchange**
   - Use familial connections to cement alliances

## Expected Outcomes
- United front against common threat
- Shared military and diplomatic burden
- Increased security through collective strength
