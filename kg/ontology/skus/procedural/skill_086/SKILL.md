# Assassination as Desperate Measure

## Overview
A last-resort strategy when facing imminent destruction by a superior force, using targeted elimination of enemy leadership.

## Prerequisites
- All conventional options exhausted
- Enemy is actively threatening existence
- No reasonable diplomatic solution available

## Steps

1. **Secretly cultivate capable agents**
   - Recruit individuals willing to undertake high-risk missions
   - Train and prepare them over time

2. **Create plausible pretext for access**
   - Use diplomatic missions as cover
   - Offer something the enemy wants (maps, tribute, intelligence)

3. **Execute the strike at close range**
   - Agent must get within striking distance of target
   - Conceal weapons within the offered gift or tribute

## Critical Warnings
- Failure results in immediate retaliation
- Enemy will likely respond with full military force
- Success is uncertain and depends on target's vigilance

## Expected Outcomes
- If successful: removes primary threat
- If failed: accelerates enemy attack and may seal your destruction
- Consider whether this truly solves the underlying power imbalance
