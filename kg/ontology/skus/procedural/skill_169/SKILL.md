# Counter-Slander Defense

## Overview
Recognize and respond to coordinated disinformation campaigns aimed at destroying your position.

## Warning Signs
- Enemies spreading gold to bribe your former associates
- Former associates suddenly speaking against you
- Foreign agents publicly celebrating your supposed rise
- Leadership hearing constant negative reports
- Questions about your loyalty appearing repeatedly

## Defensive Measures
1. Monitor for unusual patterns of criticism
2. Identify the source and funding behind campaigns
3. Maintain transparent communication with leadership
4. Document your actual actions and intentions
5. Build independent alliances that can vouch for your character

## When Defense Fails
- Recognize when trust has been irreparably damaged
- Withdraw from public life rather than force confrontation
- Preserve your remaining relationships and resources

## Expected Outcomes
- Early detection allows for countermeasures
- If unsuccessful, graceful withdrawal preserves some reputation
- Understanding the mechanism helps prevent future vulnerability
