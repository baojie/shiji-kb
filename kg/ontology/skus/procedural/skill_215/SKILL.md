# Xiongnu Legal Procedures

## Overview
The Xiongnu maintained a swift and severe legal system focused on military discipline and property rights.

## Criminal Offenses and Punishments

### Capital Crimes (Death Penalty)
- Drawing a weapon (blade) one foot (拔刃尺者死)
- Major offenses (unspecified serious crimes)

### Property Crimes
- Theft: Confiscation of the thief's family and property (坐盗者没入其家)

### Minor Offenses
- Punishment: Crushing/pressing (轧)
- Applied to lesser crimes

## Judicial Process

1. **Arrest**: Offender detained
2. **Summary judgment**: Quick determination of guilt
3. **Sentencing**: Based on severity classification

## Key Characteristics
- **Speed**: Imprisonment lasts no more than ten days (狱久者不过十日)
- **Low prison population**: Entire nation typically has only a few prisoners (一国之囚不过数人)
- **Severity**: Swift and harsh punishments serve as deterrent

## Expected Outcomes
- Rapid resolution of criminal matters
- Minimal prison infrastructure needed
- Strong deterrent through severity and speed
