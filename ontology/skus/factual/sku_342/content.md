Liu Bi (吴王濞) was the son of Liu Zhong (刘仲), who was Emperor Gaozu's brother. In the 7th year of Gaozu's reign, Liu Zhong was made King of Dai (代王), but fled when the Xiongnu attacked. Gaozu demoted him to Marquis of Heyang (郃阳侯). In autumn of the 11th year of Gaozu's reign, Ying Bu (英布), King of Huainan, rebelled. Liu Bi, then 20 years old and Marquis of Pei (沛侯), participated in crushing the rebellion. After Liu Jia, King of Jing, was killed without heirs, Gaozu appointed Liu Bi as King of Wu (吴王), ruling three commanderies with 53 cities.
