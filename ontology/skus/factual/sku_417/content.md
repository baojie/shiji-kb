## Liu Chang (淮南厉王)
- **Parentage**: Youngest son of Emperor Gaozu (Liu Bang); mother was a concubine of Zhang Ao, King of Zhao
- **Birth**: Born after his mother was imprisoned during the Bairen conspiracy; mother committed suicide after giving birth
- **Upbringing**: Raised by Empress Lü after Emperor Gaozu regretted not helping his mother
- **Title**: Made Prince of Huainan in 11th year of Gaozu (196 BCE) after Qing Bu's rebellion
- **Physical strength**: Could lift a bronze cauldron (力能扛鼎)
- **Revenge**: Killed Piyang Hou with an iron hammer, accusing him of three crimes related to his mother's death
- **Crimes**: Used imperial privileges, made his own laws, plotted rebellion with 70 men
- **Exile**: Exiled to Yan Dao in Shu Commandery; died by refusing food during transport
- **Children**: Four sons - Liu An (Fuling Marquis), Liu Bo (Anyang Marquis), Liu Ci (Yangzhou Marquis), Liu Liang (Dongcheng Marquis)
