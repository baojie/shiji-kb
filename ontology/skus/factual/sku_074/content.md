## Empress Dowager Lü (吕太后)

**Background:** Liu Bang's wife from his early days, mother of Emperor Hui and Princess Lu Yuan

**Character:** Resolute and ruthless, assisted Gaozu in eliminating many meritorious ministers

**Persecution of Consort Qi:**
- Imprisoned Consort Qi in 永巷
- Poisoned her son, King of Zhao (Liu Ruyi)
- Mutilated Consort Qi: cut off hands and feet, gouged out eyes, deafened ears, made mute with drugs, called her "Human Pig" (人彘)
- Showed her to Emperor Hui, who was so horrified he fell ill and could not govern

**Rule after Emperor Hui's Death:**
- Emperor Hui died in 7th year (188 BCE)
- Lü ruled as regent, issuing edicts in her own name
- Enfeoffed multiple Lü family members as kings, violating the oath "Those not of the Liu clan who become kings, the world shall attack them"

**Lü Kings:**
- 吕台 (Lü Tai): King of Lü
- 吕产 (Lü Chan): King of Lü (later King of Liang)
- 吕禄 (Lü Lu): King of Zhao
- 吕通 (Lü Tong): King of Yan

**Downfall of Lü Clan (180 BCE):**
- After Empress Lü's death, ministers led by Zhou Bo and Chen Ping acted
- Liu Zhang (朱虚侯) killed Lü Chan
- Lü Lu was captured and killed
- Lü Xu was beaten to death
- All Lü family members were executed regardless of age

**Succession:** Ministers chose Liu Heng, King of Dai, as Emperor Wen
