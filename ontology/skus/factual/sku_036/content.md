## 帝辛(纣)的暴政

**Character**: Intelligent, strong (could fight wild animals with bare hands), but used his wit to reject advice and cover up wrongdoings.

**Excesses**:
- Loved wine and pleasure; favored 妲己
- Created new licentious music and dances
- Heavily taxed people to fill 鹿台 with money and 钜桥 with grain
- Collected exotic animals at 沙丘苑台
- Disrespected spirits and gods
- Created 酒池肉林 - wine pool and meat forest - for orgies

**Cruel punishments**:
- Created 砲格之刑 - burning on heated bronze pillar
- Executed 九侯 and made him into mincemeat (醢)
- Executed 鄂侯 and made him into dried meat (脯)
- Imprisoned 西伯昌 at 羑里
- Cut open 比干's heart to see if sages had seven openings
- Imprisoned 箕子

**Fall**: When 周武王 attacked, 纣's soldiers turned against him. 纣 burned himself at 鹿台.
