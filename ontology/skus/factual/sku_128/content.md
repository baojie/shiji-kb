## Geography and Culture of Qi

### Sima Qian's Description

**Geography**:
- From Mount Tai (泰山) to Langya (琅邪)
- North extending to the sea
- Fertile soil extending 2,000 li (膏壤二千里)

**People's Character**:
- Broad-minded and open (阔达)
- Often hiding their wisdom (多匿知)
- Considered their natural disposition

**Historical Assessment**:
- With Taigong Wang's sage wisdom establishing the foundation
- Duke Huan's prosperity and good governance
- Hosting feudal lord assemblies and becoming hegemon - was this not fitting?
- "Vast indeed! Truly the style of a great state!" (洋洋哉，固大国之风也)
