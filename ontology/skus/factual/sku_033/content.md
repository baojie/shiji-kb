## 襃姒 Legend

**Origin**: 
- Two dragons appeared at 夏 court, leaving behind saliva (漦)
- This was preserved in a box through 夏, 商, and 周 dynasties
- 厉王 opened it; the saliva flowed out and couldn't be removed
- It turned into a black turtle and entered the harem
- A young palace maid encountered it; she became pregnant years later without a husband
- She abandoned the child; it was rescued by a couple selling 檿弧箕服
- This child became 襃姒

**幽王's obsession**:
- 幽王 loved 襃姒 but she never smiled
- He lit beacon fires to summon feudal lords; when they arrived with no enemy, 襃姒 laughed
- He repeated this until lords stopped coming

**Fall**: 幽王 deposed Queen 申 and Crown Prince 宜臼 in favor of 襃姒 and her son 伯服. 申侯 allied with 缯 and 犬戎 to attack. 幽王 lit beacon fires but no lords came. He was killed at 骊山.
