**Left (Eastern) Region:** Left-side kings and generals lived in the east, facing Shanggu commandery, bordering the Huimo (秽貉) and Chaoxian (Korea).

**Right (Western) Region:** Right-side kings and generals lived in the west, facing Shangjun commandery and beyond, bordering the Yuezhi, Di, and Qiang peoples.

**Center:** The Chanyu's court faced Dai and Yunzhong commanderies.

Each group had designated territories and migrated following water and grass.
