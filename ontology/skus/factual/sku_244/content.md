**Mencius (孟轲)**
- Origin: Zou (驺)
- Education: Studied under a disciple of Zisi (子思)
- Travels: Served King Xuan of Qi (advice not used); went to Liang, King Hui found his words impractical
- Historical context: Era when states pursued military alliances (合从连衡); Mencius spoke of virtue of Yao, Shun, and Three Dynasties
- Works: Retired with Wan Zhang and others to arrange the Odes and Documents, wrote 7 chapters of Mencius (孟子七篇)
