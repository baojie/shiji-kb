## Zhang Qian (张骞)
- **Origin**: Hanzhong
- **Mission**: Sent by Emperor Wu to find the Yuezhi and form alliance against Xiongnu in Jianyuan era (140-135 BCE)
- **Captivity**: Captured by Xiongnu for over 10 years; married and had children but kept the Han imperial tally
- **Escape**: Reached Dayuan via Kangju; found Yuezhi content in their new land with no desire for revenge
- **Return**: Captured again by Xiongnu; escaped during succession crisis
- **Duration**: 13 years; only 2 of 100+ companions survived
- **Title**: Made Marquis of Bowang

## Western Regions Geography
- **Dayuan**: West of Xiongnu, 10,000 li from Han; 70+ cities; produces "sweating blood" horses
- **Kangju**: Northwest of Dayuan, 2,000 li; 80,000-90,000 archers
- **Yuezhi**: West of Dayuan 2,000-3,000 li; 100,000-200,000 archers; moved west after defeat by Xiongnu
- **Anxi (Parthia)**: West of Yuezhi; hundreds of cities; uses silver coins with king's image
- **Daxia (Bactria)**: Southwest of Dayuan; 1 million people; weak military; conquered by Yuezhi
- **Shendu (India)**: Southeast of Daxia; produces bamboo and cloth from Shu

## Expedition to Dayuan
- **Cause**: Dayuan refused to give "heavenly horses" and killed Han envoy
- **First expedition (104 BCE)**: Li Guangli led 6,000 cavalry and tens of thousands of young men; failed due to hunger
- **Second expedition (102 BCE)**: 60,000 soldiers, 100,000 laborers; besieged Dayuan capital
- **Result**: Dayuan nobles killed their king and surrendered; Han obtained 3,000+ horses
- **Cost**: Only 10,000 men returned; expedition lasted 4 years
