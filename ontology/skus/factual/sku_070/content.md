## Hongmen Banquet (鸿门宴)

**Date:** After Liu Bang entered Guanzhong first

**Forces:**
- Xiang Yu: 400,000 troops at Hongmen, Xin'an
- Liu Bang: 100,000 troops at Bashang

**Key Figures:**
- 曹无伤 (Cao Wushang): Liu Bang's Left Sima who informed Xiang Yu of Liu Bang's ambitions
- 范增 (Fan Zeng): Xiang Yu's advisor (Yafu/亚父), urged Xiang Yu to kill Liu Bang
- 项伯 (Xiang Bo): Xiang Yu's uncle, friend of Zhang Liang, warned Liu Bang
- 张良 (Zhang Liang): Liu Bang's advisor
- 樊哙 (Fan Kuai): Liu Bang's general who famously ate raw pork shoulder
- 项庄 (Xiang Zhuang): Performed sword dance attempting to kill Liu Bang

**Outcome:** Liu Bang escaped under pretense of using the latrine, returned to his camp, and executed Cao Wushang

**Famous Quote from Fan Zeng:** "竖子不足与谋! 夺项王天下者，必沛公也" (This brat is not worth plotting with! The one who will take Xiang Yu's realm must be the Duke of Pei!)
