At Changping, Zhao lost a commander (都尉). Yu Qing advised sending envoys with treasure to Chu and Wei to make Qin fear an alliance. The King didn't listen, instead sending Zheng Zhu (郑硃) to Qin for peace talks. Qin publicly honored Zheng Zhu to show other states that Zhao was seeking peace, causing Chu and Wei not to send aid. Zhao suffered a crushing defeat at Changping, and Qin besieged Handan, becoming a laughingstock of the world.
