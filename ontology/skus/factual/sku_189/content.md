## Wei Clan Origins

- **Ancestor**: Bi Gong Gao (毕公高), descendant shared the same surname as the Zhou dynasty
- **Enfeoffment**: After King Wu defeated Zhou (Shang), Gao was enfeoffed at Bi (毕), thus taking Bi as his surname
- **Later status**: The title was eventually discontinued, descendants became commoners, some living in China proper, others among barbarian tribes
- **Bi Wan (毕万)**: A descendant who served Duke Xian of Jin (晋献公)
- **Wei fief**: In the 16th year of Duke Xian, Bi Wan served as right charioteer in the campaign against Huo, Geng, and Wei. After conquering them, Bi Wan was enfeoffed at Wei and made a Daifu (大夫)
- **Prophecy**: Diviner Yan predicted Bi Wan's descendants would prosper greatly, as "Wan" means "full number" and "Wei" means "great name"
- **Surname change**: Bi Wan's descendants adopted Wei as their clan name from the fief
