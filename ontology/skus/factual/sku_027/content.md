## Shang Capital Relocations

- From 契 to 汤: **Eight relocations**
- 成汤: First resided at **亳**
- 中丁: Moved to **隞**
- 河亶甲: Resided at **相**
- 祖乙: Moved to **邢**
- 盘庚: Crossed Yellow River south, returned to 成汤's old capital at **亳** (fifth relocation)
- 武乙: Moved north of Yellow River

盘庚's relocation was met with resistance from the people, but he convinced them by appealing to 成汤's legacy.
