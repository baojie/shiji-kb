## Shi She (石奢)
- **Position**: Prime Minister of Chu under King Zhao
- **Incident**: While touring, encountered his father committing murder; let father go and turned himself in
- **Death**: Committed suicide rather than accept king's pardon, stating he couldn't be both unfilial and disloyal

## Li Li (李离)
- **Position**: Judge under Duke Wen of Jin
- **Incident**: Wrongly sentenced someone to death through mistaken judgment
- **Death**: Committed suicide by sword rather than accept duke's pardon, stating judges must accept punishment for their errors
