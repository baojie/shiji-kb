**First Campaign:** Li Guangli (Ershi General) led 30,000 cavalry from Jiuquan against the Right Wise King at Tianshan, capturing over 10,000 heads. However, he was nearly trapped by Xiongnu forces and lost 60-70% of his men.

**Second Campaign:** Led 60,000 cavalry and 100,000 infantry from Shuofang. The Chanyu moved his baggage north of the Yuwu River and waited with 100,000 cavalry south of the river. After fighting for over ten days, Li Guangli heard his family had been executed due to a witchcraft scandal and surrendered to the Xiongnu with his remaining forces. Only one or two out of a thousand returned.

**Aftermath:** The Taiyi Ling Sui Dan was arrested for revealing that Li Guangli's family had been wiped out, which contributed to his surrender.
