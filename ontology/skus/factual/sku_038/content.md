## Eastern Zhou Political Decline

**平王 era**: Zhou declined; feudal lords grew powerful; 齐、楚、秦、晋 began to dominate; politics controlled by regional hegemons (方伯).

**Internal conflicts**:
- 子朝 vs 悼王/敬王 succession struggle
- 东西周分治 during 赧王's time

**Qin's rise**:
- 显王5年: Congratulated 秦献公 who claimed hegemony
- 显王9年: Sent sacrificial meat to 秦孝公
- 显王26年: Acknowledged 秦孝公 as hegemon
- 显王44年: 秦惠王 declared himself King
- 威烈王23年: Recognized 韩、魏、赵 as feudal lords (Three Jin)

**End of Zhou**: 赧王59年, 秦 conquered 西周, took 36 cities and 30,000 people. Seven years later, 秦庄襄王 conquered 东周.
