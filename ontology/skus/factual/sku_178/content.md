Fan Li sailed to Qi, changed his name to Chiyi Zipi (鸱夷子皮), and farmed by the sea, accumulating hundreds of thousands in wealth. The people of Qi, hearing of his virtue, made him prime minister. He sighed: 'At home I accumulated a thousand gold; in office I reached prime minister - this is the peak for a commoner. Long enjoyment of such honor is inauspicious.' He returned the seal, distributed his wealth, and left with precious treasures to Tao (陶), calling it the center of the world for trade. He called himself Tao Zhu Gong (陶硃公). Through farming, animal husbandry, and trading at the right times for 10% profit, he accumulated millions. The world called him Tao Zhu Gong.
