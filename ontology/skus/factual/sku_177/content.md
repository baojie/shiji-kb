After Yue's victory, Fan Li left and sent a letter to Wen Zhong stating: 'When the birds are gone, the good bow is stored; when the cunning rabbit is dead, the hunting dog is cooked. King Goujian has a long neck and a bird's beak - he can be shared in hardship but not in pleasure. Why don't you leave?' Wen Zhong claimed illness and stopped attending court. Someone slandered him as plotting rebellion. Goujian gave him a sword saying: 'You taught me seven strategies to defeat Wu. I used three and succeeded. The remaining four are with you - try them with my late father.' Wen Zhong committed suicide.
