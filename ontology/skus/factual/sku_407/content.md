**Military Losses in Yuan Shou Year 4 (元狩四年)**

- **Horses deployed**: 140,000 total (official and private horses counted at the border)
- **Horses returned**: Less than 30,000

After this campaign, the position of Grand Marshal (大司马) was created, with both Grand General Wei Qing and Cavalry General Huo Qubing appointed to this rank. Their salaries and ranks were made equal. Thereafter, Wei Qing's influence declined while Huo Qubing grew increasingly favored.
