**Destruction of Donghu:** Modu initially appeared submissive to the Donghu, giving them a famous thousand-li horse and then one of his favorite consorts when demanded. When Donghu demanded abandoned border land, Modu angrily declared 'Land is the foundation of a state - how can we give it away!' and executed those who suggested giving it. He then launched a surprise attack on the unprepared Donghu, destroying their king and capturing their people and livestock.

**Western Campaigns:** Defeated and drove away the Yuezhi, annexed the Loufan and Baiyang kings south of the Yellow River.

**Recovery of Territory:** Reclaimed all lands previously taken by Qin general Meng Tian, establishing borders with Han at the old southern fortresses, reaching Chao-na and Fu-shi, then invading Yan and Dai.
