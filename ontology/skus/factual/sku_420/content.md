## Sun Shu'ao (孙叔敖)
- **Position**: Prime Minister of Chu under King Zhuang
- **Governance**: Within three months, achieved harmony between upper and lower classes
- **Currency reform**: When king changed coins, caused market chaos; Sun advised reversal within five days
- **Carriage reform**: Instead of ordering higher carriages, suggested raising gate thresholds; people raised carriages voluntarily within six months
- **Philosophy**: Did not rejoice at gaining position three times, nor regret losing it three times
