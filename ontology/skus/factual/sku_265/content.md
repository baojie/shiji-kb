After Qin lifted the siege of Handan, King Zhao considered ceding six counties to Qin for peace. Yu Qing argued against it, proposing instead to give the six counties to Qi (Qin's enemy). This would cause Qin to seek peace with Zhao. The plan succeeded - before Yu Qing even returned from Qi, Qin's envoy was already in Zhao seeking peace. Lou Huan (楼缓) fled, and Yu Qing was enfeoffed with a city.
