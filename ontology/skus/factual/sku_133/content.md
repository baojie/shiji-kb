## 三桓 (Three Huan Families)

**Origin:** Descendants of the three brothers of Duke Zhuang:
- **季氏 (Ji clan)** - Descended from Ji You (季友/成季), youngest brother
- **孟氏 (Meng clan)** - Descended from Qing Fu (庆父), eldest brother
- **叔孙氏 (Shusun clan)** - Descended from Shu Ya (叔牙), middle brother

**Rise to Power:**
- Began after Xiang Zhong killed legitimate heirs to install Duke Xuan
- By Duke Zhao's time, controlled Lu's government
- Year 11 of Duke Xiang: Divided Lu's army into three, each family controlling one

**Key Events:**
- Duke Zhao attempted to eliminate Ji Ping Zi but was defeated by combined Three Huan forces
- Duke Zhao died in exile after 32 years
- Yang Hu attempted to kill Three Huan heirs and replace them (year 8 of Duke Ding)
- By Duke Dao's reign: Three Huan completely dominated; Lu ruler reduced to status of minor vassal

**Ji Wen Zi (季文子):** Served as prime minister for three rulers; known for integrity - no silk-clad concubines, no grain-fed horses, no gold or jade in treasury
