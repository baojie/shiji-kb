After conquering Wu, Goujian led his army north across the Huai River and met with feudal lords of Qi and Jin at Xuzhou (徐州). He presented tribute to the Zhou court. King Yuan of Zhou sent ritual meat (胙) and bestowed the title of Hegemon (伯). Goujian gave Huai lands to Chu, returned Song's conquered lands, and gave 100 li east of the Si River to Lu. At this time, Yue dominated the region between the Yangtze and Huai Rivers. All feudal lords congratulated him, and he was called Hegemon King (霸王).
