**Huo Qubing (霍去病)** - Cavalry General of Han Dynasty

- **Relation**: Nephew of Wei Qing (son of Wei Qing's sister Shao'er)
- **Age when active**: 18 years old when first appointed
- **Position**: Attendant of the Emperor (侍中), then Colonel of Piao Yao (剽姚校尉)
- **Character**: Few words, kept secrets, bold and decisive; said "The Xiongnu are not yet destroyed, how can I think of a home?"
- **Military style**: Dared to penetrate deep into enemy territory, often led elite cavalry ahead of main force
- **Death**: Died in Yuan Shou Year 6 (元狩六年), three years after his Yuan Shou Year 4 campaign
- **Posthumous title**: Jing Huan Hou (景桓侯)
- **Burial**: Tomb shaped like Qilian Mountain, funeral procession from Chang'an to Maoling with armored soldiers
- **Son**: Huo Shan (嬗), courtesy name Zihou (子侯), died in Yuan Feng Year 1 (元封元年), title: Ai Hou (哀侯), no heir, title extinguished
