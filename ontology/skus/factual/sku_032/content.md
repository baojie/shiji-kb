## 共和 Regency (841-828 BCE)

**Background**: 厉王 was tyrannical and suppressed criticism using a witch to monitor and execute critics. People rebelled and 厉王 fled to 彘.

**Regency**: 召公 and 周公 jointly administered the state, called "共和".

**Duration**: 14 years

**Resolution**: After 厉王 died at 彘, the two ministers enthroned his son 静 as 宣王.

**Historical significance**: The year 841 BCE (first year of Gonghe) is traditionally considered the first reliable date in Chinese history.
