After being pardoned by Wu, Goujian returned to Yue and subjected himself to great hardship. He placed a gall bladder (胆) by his seat, tasting it whenever he sat, lay down, ate, or drank, saying 'Have you forgotten the shame of Huiji?' He personally farmed while his wife wove. He ate no extra meat, wore no layered colored clothes, humbled himself before wise men, treated guests generously, and shared labor with the common people. He entrusted domestic governance to Minister Wen Zhong while Fan Li served as hostage in Wu for two years.
