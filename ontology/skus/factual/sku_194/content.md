## Li Kui's Five Criteria for Judging Character

When Marquis Wen asked Li Kui how to choose between Wei Chengzi and Zhai Huang as prime minister, Li Kui provided five criteria:

1. **居视其所亲** - When at rest, observe whom he associates with
2. **富视其所与** - When wealthy, observe to whom he gives
3. **达视其所举** - When successful, observe whom he recommends
4. **穷视其所不为** - When in difficulty, observe what he will not do
5. **贫视其所不取** - When poor, observe what he will not take

Li Kui concluded: "These five are sufficient to determine him, why wait for me?"

This led to Wei Chengzi being appointed prime minister.
