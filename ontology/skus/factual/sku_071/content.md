## Xiang Yu's Death

**Battle of Gaixia (垓下之战):**
- Han forces: 300,000 under Han Xin
- Xiang Yu's forces: approximately 100,000
- Han forces surrounded Xiang Yu's camp
- Famous "Four sides all Chu songs" (四面楚歌) - Han troops sang Chu songs to demoralize Xiang Yu's army

**Xiang Yu's Final Poem (垓下歌):**
"力拔山兮气盖世，时不利兮骓不逝。骓不逝兮可柰何，虞兮虞兮柰若何！"
(Power to pull mountains, spirit to cover the world; but the times are against me, and Zhui my horse will not run. What can I do? Yu, my lady, what will become of you!)

**Escape and Death:**
- Escaped with 800 horsemen at night
- Only 100+ remained after crossing the Huai River
- Lost his way at Yinling, misled by a farmer
- Reached Dongcheng with only 28 riders
- Fought his way to Wujiang River
- Refused to cross, saying he had no face to see his江东 elders after losing 8,000 men
- Committed suicide by cutting his throat

**Those who received his body parts (all enfeoffed):**

- 杨喜 (Yang Xi): 赤泉侯

- 吕胜 (Lv Sheng): 涅阳侯
