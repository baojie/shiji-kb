**Other Warring States Thinkers**

**Gongsun Long (公孙龙)** - Zhao, known for debates on "Hard and White" (坚白同异之辩)

**Ju Zi (剧子)** - Zhao

**Li Kui (李悝)** - Wei, taught "exhausting the land's productivity" (尽地力之教)

**Shi Zi (尸子)** - Chu

**Chang Lu (长卢)** - Chu

**Yu Zi (吁子)** - From E (阿)

**Mo Di (墨翟)** - Song, served as official, skilled in defense, advocated frugality. Some say he was contemporary with Confucius, others say after.
