After the rebellion: The kingdoms of Jiaoxi, Jiaodong, Zichuan, and Jinan were abolished and incorporated into Han. The King of Jibei, having been forcibly restrained by his officials from joining the rebellion, was spared and relocated to become King of Zichuan. Liu Li, younger son of Prince Yuan of Chu, was made King of Chu. Liu Fei, King of Runan, was relocated to the former Wu territory as King of Jiangdu.
