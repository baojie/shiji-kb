## Dongfang Shuo (东方朔)
- **Origin**: Qi
- **Entry to court**: Submitted 3,000 bamboo slips; took Emperor Wu two months to read
- **Position**: Gentleman of the Palace; frequently attended the emperor
- **Behavior**: Called "madman" by colleagues; claimed to be "hiding from the world within the court"
- **Lifestyle**: Frequently married and divorced young women of Chang'an; spent all imperial gifts on them
- **Predictions**: Correctly predicted the Xiongnu Hunye King's surrender; claimed to recognize the "Zou Ya" beast
- **Deathbed advice**: Warned emperor to avoid clever flatterers and slanderers
- **Philosophy**: Compared himself to ancient sages who hid in mountains; said he hid in the palace instead
