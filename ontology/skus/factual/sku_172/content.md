In the first year of King Goujian's reign, King Helü of Wu attacked Yue after learning of Yunchang's death. Goujian sent death-defying warriors who charged to the Wu formation in three rows, shouted, and cut their own throats. While the Wu army watched in shock, Yue launched a surprise attack. The Wu army was defeated at Zuili (槜李), and King Helü was shot and wounded. Before dying, Helü told his son Fuchai: 'Do not forget Yue.'
