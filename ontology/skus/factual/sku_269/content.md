Pingyuan Lord's tower overlooked a commoner's home where a cripple (躄者) lived. A beauty in Pingyuan Lord's household laughed at the cripple from above. The cripple demanded the head of the beauty who laughed at him, arguing that Pingyuan Lord valued scholars over concubines. Pingyuan Lord initially refused, calling it excessive. Over half his guests left. When asked why, one explained they saw him valuing beauty over scholars. Pingyuan Lord then killed the beauty and presented her head to the cripple. Guests gradually returned.
