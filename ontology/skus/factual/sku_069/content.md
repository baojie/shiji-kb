## Battle of Julu (钜鹿之战)

**Context:** Qin general Zhang Han besieged Zhao at Julu after defeating Xiang Liang at Dingtao

**Xiang Yu's Tactics:**
- After executing Song Yi (who delayed for 46 days), Xiang Yu took command
- Crossed the river and ordered: "Sink all boats, break all cooking pots, burn all shelters, carry only three days' provisions" (破釜沉舟)
- Demonstrated determination to fight to the death with no retreat

**Battle Results:**
- Nine engagements with Qin forces
- Cut off Qin supply lines (甬道)
- Killed Qin general 苏角 (Su Jiao)
- Captured Qin general 王离 (Wang Li)
- 涉间 (She Jian) refused to surrender and burned himself to death

- Xiang Yu became Supreme General of all诸侯 forces
