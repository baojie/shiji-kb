## Hengshan King Liu Ci (衡山王赐)
- **Relation**: Brother of Huainan King Liu An
- **Family conflicts**: Queen Cheng Shu died; Xu Lai became queen; rivalry between Crown Prince Shuang and son Xiao
- **Rebellion**: Plotted with Huainan King; manufactured weapons, forged imperial seals
- **Discovery**: Exposed when Chen Xi was found at son Xiao's home
- **Death**: Committed suicide; Queen Xu Lai, Crown Prince Shuang, and Xiao all executed
