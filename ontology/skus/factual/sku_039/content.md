## Qin Mythological Origins

The Qin people traced their ancestry to **Emperor Zhuanxu** (颛顼). The lineage begins with **Nüxiu** (女脩), a descendant of Zhuanxu. According to legend, while Nüxiu was weaving, a dark bird (玄鸟) dropped an egg, which she swallowed and subsequently gave birth to **Daye** (大业).

Daye married **Nühua** (女华), daughter of Shaodian. Nühua gave birth to **Dafei** (大费), who assisted Yu the Great in controlling the floods. For this service, Emperor Shun bestowed upon Dafei the surname **Ying** (嬴) and gave him a jade woman of the Yao clan as wife. Dafei, also known as **Boyi** (柏翳), helped Shun tame birds and beasts.
