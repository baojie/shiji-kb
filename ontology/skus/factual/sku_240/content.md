**Gan Luo (甘罗)**
- Relation: Grandson of Gan Mao
- Age: 12 years old when serving Lü Buwei (文信侯)
- Famous deeds:
  - Convinced Zhang Tang to accept mission to Yan by warning him of Wu'an Jun's fate
  - Negotiated with Zhao: secured 5 cities for Qin to expand Hejian region
  - Zhao then attacked Yan, gained 30 cities, gave 11 to Qin
- Reward: Appointed Shangqing (上卿 - high official), given Gan Mao's former fields and residences
