**Death of First Emperor**
- **Date**: 7th month, Bingyin day, Year 37 (210 BCE)
- **Location**: Shaqiu Platform (沙丘平台)
- **Cause**: Illness during inspection tour
- **Last Edict**: Letter to Crown Prince Fusu to return to Xianyang for funeral

**Succession Conspiracy**
- Zhao Gao and Li Si concealed the death
- Body kept in cooling carriage with salted fish to mask odor
- Original edict to Fusu destroyed
- Fake edict created naming Hu Hai as heir
- Fusu and Meng Tian ordered to commit suicide
- Hu Hai became Second Emperor (Er Shi)

**Burial at Lishan**
- Construction began at accession, intensified after unification
- 700,000+ laborers used
- Features: Three underground springs penetrated, bronze-lined coffin, mercury rivers, astronomical/terrestrial representations, automatic crossbows, whale-oil eternal lamps
- Concubines without children ordered to accompany in death
