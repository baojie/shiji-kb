## Battle of Muye (牧野之战)

**Date**: 甲子日, 2nd month, 11th year of 武王

**Zhou Forces**:
- 300 chariots (戎车三百乘)
- 3,000 elite warriors (虎贲三千人)
- 45,000 armored soldiers (甲士四万五千人)
- 800 feudal lords joined at 盟津
- Allied peoples: 庸、蜀、羌、髳、微、纑、彭、濮

**Shang Forces**:
- 700,000 soldiers (七十万人)

**Outcome**: Shang soldiers had no will to fight; they turned their weapons around to open the way for 武王. 纣 fled to 鹿台, dressed in precious jade, and burned himself to death.

**Aftermath**: 武王 cut off 纣's head and displayed it on a white banner. 妲己 was executed. 箕子 was released from prison. 比干's tomb was honored. 商容's gate was marked. 纣's son 武庚/禄父 was enfeoffed to continue Shang sacrifices.
