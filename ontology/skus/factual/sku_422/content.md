## Gongyi Xiu (公仪休)
- **Position**: Prime Minister of Lu
- **Principle**: Officials should not compete with common people for profit
- **Refused fish**: When offered fish by a visitor, refused saying if he accepted fish he might lose his position and then couldn't afford fish
- **Destroyed vegetable garden**: Pulled up his garden vegetables to avoid competing with farmers
- **Dismissed wife**: Sent away his weaving wife and burned the loom to avoid competing with weavers
