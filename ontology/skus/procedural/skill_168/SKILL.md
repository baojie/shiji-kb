# Responding to Homeland Crisis

## Overview
Set aside personal conflicts and past grievances when your homeland or core community faces destruction.

## Steps
1. Receive news of threat to homeland
2. Acknowledge any personal grievances with leadership
3. Assess whether personal pride outweighs collective survival
4. Recognize that your value and reputation depend on the survival of your community
5. Immediately set aside prohibitions or warnings against return
6. Mobilize resources and return without delay
7. Accept reconciliation with former adversaries for the greater good

## Decision Points
- The moment you understand that your identity and worth are tied to your community's survival, all personal grievances become irrelevant

## Expected Outcomes
- Preservation of community and its institutions
- Personal redemption and restored standing
- Demonstration of character that enhances long-term reputation
