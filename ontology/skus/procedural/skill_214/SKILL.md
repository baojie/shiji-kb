# Xiongnu Seasonal Governance Rituals

## Overview
The Xiongnu maintained a structured annual calendar of gatherings for religious observance, governance, and resource assessment.

## Annual Schedule

### First Month (正月)
- **Event**: Small gathering at Shanyu's court (单于庭)
- **Purpose**: Sacrificial ceremonies (祠)
- **Participants**: Tribal leaders and nobles

### Fifth Month (五月)
- **Event**: Grand assembly at Longcheng (茏城)
- **Purpose**: Worship ancestors, heaven, earth, and spirits (祭其先、天地、鬼神)
- **Scale**: Major ceremonial gathering

### Autumn (秋)
- **Event**: Grand assembly at Dailin (蹛林)
- **Purpose**: Census and resource assessment (课校人畜计)
- **Activities**: Count people and livestock for taxation and planning

## Daily Rituals
- **Morning**: Shanyu exits camp and bows to the rising sun
- **Evening**: Bows to the moon

## Meeting Protocols
- Seating: Elders on the left, facing north
- Auspicious days: Wu and Ji days (戊己)

## Expected Outcomes
- Religious observance maintained
- Accurate population and livestock records
- Tribal cohesion through shared ceremonies
