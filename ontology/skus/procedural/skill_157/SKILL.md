# Self-Recommendation Strategy

## Overview
A procedure for advocating for inclusion in important missions when you have been overlooked, using the "point in a sheath" metaphor.

## Steps

1. **Identify the opportunity** - Learn of a mission requiring personnel
2. **Assess the gap** - Determine if there is an unfilled position
3. **Approach the decision-maker directly** - Request inclusion to fill the gap
4. **Anticipate rejection based on track record** - Prepare for skepticism about your abilities
5. **Reframe the narrative** - Argue that lack of opportunity, not lack of ability, explains obscurity
6. **Use the sheath metaphor** - Explain that talent only becomes visible when given opportunity (like a point in a sheath)
7. **Request a chance to prove yourself** - Ask to be placed where your abilities can emerge

## Key Argument
"I have not yet been given the opportunity to demonstrate my abilities. Place me in a position where I can act, and my true capabilities will emerge fully."

## Expected Outcome
Inclusion in the mission, opportunity to demonstrate capabilities, eventual recognition of talent
