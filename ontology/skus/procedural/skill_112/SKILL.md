# Wealth Relocation Strategy

## Overview
A procedure for restarting life and building wealth in a new location, demonstrated by Fan Li's transformation into Tao Zhu Gong.

## Steps

1. **Leave with portable wealth** - Take only light valuables (jewels, precious items)
2. **Change identity** - Adopt new name (Fan Li became "Chiyi Zipi")
3. **Choose strategic location** - Find "center of the world" with good trade routes
4. **Start with honest labor** - Farm and work alongside family
5. **Practice trade timing** - Buy low, sell high; observe market cycles
6. **Accept modest profits** - Target 10% margin rather than excessive gains
7. **Redistribute when too successful** - Share wealth with friends and community
8. **Leave again if too famous** - Resign positions, disperse wealth, relocate

## Core Principle
"Living at home, one accumulates a thousand gold; serving in office, one reaches minister level - this is the peak for a commoner. Long enjoyment of such fame is inauspicious."

## Expected Outcomes
- Sustainable wealth accumulation
- Avoidance of political danger
- Reputation for wisdom and generosity
