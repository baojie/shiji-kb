# Qin Standardization System

A comprehensive procedure for implementing uniform standards across a unified multi-state empire.

## Overview
To ensure effective governance of newly unified territories, establish uniform standards for measurements, writing, and infrastructure.

## Steps

1. **Standardize weights and measures**:
   - Unify all measuring instruments (衡石丈尺)
   - Ensure consistent commercial and administrative transactions

2. **Standardize writing system**:
   - Implement uniform script (书同文字)
   - Eliminate regional writing variations

3. **Standardize transportation**:
   - Establish uniform axle widths (车同轨)
   - Build roads to standard specifications

4. **Implement Five Elements theory**:
   - Determine dynasty's elemental virtue (Qin = Water)
   - Adjust calendar to begin in tenth month
   - Adopt black as imperial color
   - Use six as the sacred number:
     - Official caps: 6 inches
     - Carriage width: 6 feet
     - Step length: 6 feet
     - Horses per carriage: 6

5. **Rename rivers**: Rename Yellow River to 'Virtue River' (德水)

6. **Rename populace**: Call common people '黔首' (black-headed ones)

## Expected Outcomes
- Unified commercial practices
- Consistent administrative documentation
- Efficient transportation network
- Coherent ideological framework
