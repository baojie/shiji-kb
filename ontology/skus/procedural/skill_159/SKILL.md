# Crisis Resource Redistribution

## Overview
A procedure for leaders to maintain morale and effectiveness during extreme crisis (siege, famine, disaster) by redistributing personal resources and demonstrating solidarity.

## Steps

1. **Acknowledge the disparity** - Recognize the gap between leader comfort and follower suffering
2. **Calculate the stakes** - Understand that if the organization falls, personal wealth becomes meaningless
3. **Redistribute luxury resources** - Share food, clothing, and comfort items with those in need
4. **Personal involvement** - Have family members participate directly in support roles
5. **Commit all resources** - Distribute personal wealth to support the collective effort
6. **Form elite units** - Use redistributed resources to attract and equip dedicated volunteers
7. **Lead from the front** - Join or support the most dangerous operations personally

## Key Insight
"If the state is destroyed, how will you keep your wealth? If the state survives, what do you lack?"

## Decision Framework
- Current comfort + organizational failure = total loss
- Personal sacrifice + organizational survival = future recovery

## Expected Outcome
Boosted morale, formation of dedicated fighting units, potential relief of crisis conditions
