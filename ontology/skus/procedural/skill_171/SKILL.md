# Strategic Exfiltration Plan

A method for extracting important personnel from hostile detention using disguise and misdirection.

## Steps

1. **Assess the stakes** - Determine what happens if extraction fails (e.g., succession crisis)
2. **Identify the window** - Find a legitimate reason for movement (e.g., envoy departure)
3. **Create the disguise** - Transform the VIP into a servant role (e.g., carriage driver)
4. **Establish the cover story** - Prepare a plausible explanation for the VIP's absence (e.g., illness)
5. **Execute the extraction** - VIP departs with legitimate travelers in disguise
6. **Maintain the deception** - Continue the cover story until extraction is confirmed
7. **Accept consequences** - The facilitator remains behind and accepts potential punishment
8. **Leverage relationships** - Use allies to advocate for leniency after extraction is complete

## Key Principle
The facilitator must be willing to sacrifice themselves to ensure the VIP's escape.

## Expected Outcomes
- VIP reaches safety
- Facilitator may face punishment but often gains respect for loyalty
