# Strict Military Organization

## Overview
A contrasting leadership approach that emphasizes strict organization, formal procedures, and constant vigilance. While more demanding on soldiers, it ensures readiness and prevents enemy exploitation of weaknesses.

## Core Principles

### 1. Maintain Strict Formations
- Organize units with clear hierarchy and structure
- Maintain proper military formations at all times
- Regular drilling and inspection

### 2. Implement Constant Vigilance
- Post regular night watches and patrols
- Sound alarm signals (watchman's drums/gongs) throughout the night
- Maintain active guard rotation

### 3. Detailed Administration
- Keep thorough military records
- Document all personnel, equipment, and supplies
- Maintain clear chain of command documentation

### 4. Enforce Discipline
- Hold soldiers to strict standards
- Limit personal freedom in camp
- Prioritize readiness over comfort

## Trade-offs
- Soldiers experience more stress and less rest
- Higher administrative overhead
- Enemy cannot easily exploit organizational weaknesses
- Forces remain prepared for sudden attacks

## Expected Outcomes
- No surprise attacks succeed
- Clear accountability for all personnel and resources
- Forces remain combat-ready at all times
- Enemy respects the disciplined formation
