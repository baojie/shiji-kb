# Recruiting and Honoring Talent

## Overview
Attract and retain capable individuals by demonstrating genuine respect, regardless of their current status or position.

## Steps
1. Identify individuals with valuable skills or knowledge, including those in humble positions
2. Approach them personally with genuine respect rather than delegating to subordinates
3. Offer material support appropriately; accept refusal with grace if they value integrity
4. Host public gatherings where you can demonstrate honor toward the individual
5. Personally travel to meet them, showing willingness to inconvenience yourself
6. Endure tests of your sincerity without showing irritation
7. Publicly acknowledge their worth before peers and subordinates
8. Give them positions of honor at gatherings

## Key Principles
- Never let wealth or status create distance from talented individuals
- Accept apparent rudeness or tests as opportunities to prove sincerity
- Let others witness your respectful treatment of the individual

## Expected Outcomes
- Loyalty and dedication from recruited talent
- Enhanced reputation that attracts more capable individuals
- Access to skills and knowledge otherwise unavailable
