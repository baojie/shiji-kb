# Building Trust Before Sensitive Advice

A method for gradually building the relationship necessary to deliver sensitive or dangerous political counsel.

## Steps

1. **Demonstrate your value indirectly** - Show competence through smaller matters first
2. **Acknowledge the risk** - Explicitly state that you know your advice could lead to your death
3. **Express your motivation** - Explain that you speak from loyalty, not personal gain
4. **Reference historical parallels** - Cite examples of trusted advisors (e.g., Jiang Ziya and King Wen)
5. **Wait for explicit invitation** - Do not rush; let the leader ask repeatedly
6. **Begin with safer topics** - Address external matters before internal politics
7. **Escalate gradually** - Move to more sensitive topics as trust builds
8. **Accept personal risk** - Make clear you are willing to face consequences

## Key Principle
The advisor must demonstrate that they value the leader's success above their own safety.

## Warning
Speaking about internal politics (especially royal family matters) is the most dangerous territory and should only be attempted after significant trust is established.

## Expected Outcomes
- Leader trusts the advisor with sensitive matters
- Advisor can influence major decisions
- Advisor's position becomes secure
