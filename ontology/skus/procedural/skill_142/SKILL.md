# Counter-Intelligence Rumor Campaign

Based on Qin's strategy at Changping to replace Lian Po with Zhao Kuo.

## Overview
Spread rumors through enemy channels to influence their command decisions.

## Steps

1. **Identify Target**
   - Find capable enemy commander (e.g., Lian Po who used defensive tactics)
   - Identify their less capable replacement (e.g., Zhao Kuo, theoretical but inexperienced)

2. **Plant Disinformation**
   - Spread word that you fear only the replacement
   - Claim the current commander is easy to defeat
   - Suggest the current commander is about to surrender

3. **Exploit Enemy Weaknesses**
   - Target enemy ruler's impatience with defensive tactics
   - Use their frustration with lack of progress
   - Capitalize on existing discontent

4. **Timing**
   - Launch campaign when enemy has suffered setbacks
   - Ensure rumors reach decision-makers directly

## Expected Outcomes
- Enemy replaces competent commander with inferior one
- Enemy adopts aggressive tactics that favor your strategy
- Victory becomes significantly easier
