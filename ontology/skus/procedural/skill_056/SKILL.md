# Music Governance Method (乐治方法)

This method describes how to use music as a tool for governance and moral cultivation.

## Core Principle
Music affects the heart and can transform social behavior.

## Understanding Music's Effects

### Six Emotional Responses to Music
| Emotion | Sound Quality |
|---------|---------------|
| 哀心 | 噍以杀 |
| 乐心 | 啴以缓 |
| 喜心 | 发以散 |
| 怒心 | 粗以厉 |
| 敬心 | 直以廉 |
| 爱心 | 和以柔 |

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1: Assess Current State
- 治世之音安以乐 - Peaceful times produce calm music
- 乱世之音怨以怒 - Chaotic times produce angry music
- 亡国之音哀以思 - Declining states produce sorrowful music

### Step 2: Create Appropriate Music
- Use 雅颂 for moral cultivation
- Avoid 郑卫之音
- Match music to desired social outcomes

### Step 3: Deploy Music Systematically
- In 宗庙:君臣上下同听之 → 和敬
- In 族长乡里:长幼同听之 → 和顺
- In 闺门之内:父子兄弟同听之 → 和亲

## Five Tones and Governance
| Tone | Organ | Virtue |

| 宫 | Spleen | 圣 |
| 商 | Lung | 义 |
| 角 | Liver | 仁 |

## Warning Signs
- If music becomes 淫佚 → society will decline
- If music becomes 哀而不庄 → governance is failing
- Monitor music trends to predict social changes
