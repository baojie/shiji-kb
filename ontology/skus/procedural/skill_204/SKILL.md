## Overview
A method for handling those who wronged you when you were powerless, once you regain authority and position.

## Steps
1. **Allow opportunity for flight**: If the wrongdoer flees, do not immediately pursue with violence
2. **Issue a measured threat**: Send word that failure to appear will result in severe consequences (to family/clan)
3. **Accept sincere apology**: When the person presents themselves in contrition (e.g., bare-backed), accept it
4. **Reference the past grievance lightly**: Use humor or light reference to the past insult
5. **Demonstrate magnanimity**: Treat the person well going forward

## Key Principles
- "死灰独不复然乎" (Can dead ashes not burn again?) - Acknowledge that fortunes change
- Those who insult the powerless may be valuable when circumstances reverse
- Mercy creates loyal supporters; vengeance creates enemies

## Decision Points
- If the person refuses to appear after summons → enforce consequences
- If the person shows genuine remorse → forgive and use their skills

## Expected Outcomes
- Reputation for generosity and fairness spreads
- Former adversaries become loyal supporters
- Others are encouraged to serve faithfully, knowing mercy is possible
