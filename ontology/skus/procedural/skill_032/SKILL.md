# Imperial Inspection Tour Procedure

A ritual and administrative procedure for emperors to inspect territories, demonstrate authority, and commemorate achievements.

## Overview
Imperial inspection tours (巡游) serve to demonstrate imperial presence, inspect local governance, and leave permanent records of achievements.

## Steps

1. **Plan route**: Identify key locations including:
   - Sacred mountains
   - Newly conquered territories
   - Strategic borders
   - Coastal regions

2. **Conduct sacrifices**: Perform rituals at significant sites:
   - Feng (封) sacrifice at Mount Tai summit
   - Shan (禅) sacrifice at Liangfu
   - Sacrifices to local deities

3. **Erect commemorative steles**:
   - Select prominent locations
   - Commission inscriptions praising imperial virtues
   - Document achievements and policies

4. **Engage local scholars**: Consult with local intellectuals on:
   - Ritual procedures
   - Local customs
   - Historical sites

5. **Address local issues**:
   - Grant amnesties or tax relief
   - Resolve administrative problems
   - Punish or reward local officials

6. **Document journey**: Record all activities for official history

## Expected Outcomes
- Demonstrated imperial authority across all territories
- Permanent monuments commemorating achievements
- Improved local governance through direct inspection
