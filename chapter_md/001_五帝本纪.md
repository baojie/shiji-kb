# [0] 五帝本纪

## <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>

### 兴起与征讨

[1] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>者，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">少典</span>之子，姓公孙，名曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>。生而神灵，弱而能言，幼而徇齐，长而敦敏，成而聪明。

[1.1] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>之时，<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">神农氏</span>世衰。诸侯相侵伐，暴虐百姓，而<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">神农氏</span>弗能征。於是<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>乃习用干戈，以征不享，诸侯咸来宾从。而<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蚩尤</span>最为暴，莫能伐。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">炎帝</span>欲侵陵诸侯，诸侯咸归<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>乃修德振兵，治五气，蓺五种，抚万民，度四方，教<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">熊罴</span>貔貅貙虎，以与<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">炎帝</span>战於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">阪泉之野</span>。三战，然後得其志。

[1.2] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蚩尤</span>作乱，不用帝命。於是<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>乃徵师诸侯，与<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蚩尤</span>战於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">涿鹿之野</span>，遂禽杀<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蚩尤</span>。

[1.3] 而诸侯咸尊<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕</span>为<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>，代<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">神农氏</span>，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>。天下有不顺者，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>从而征之，平者去之，披山通道，未尝宁居。

### 疆域与治理

[2]

- 东至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">海</span>，登<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">丸山</span>，及<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">岱宗</span>。
- 西至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">空桐</span>，登<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">鸡头</span>。
- 南至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">江</span>，登<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">熊</span>、<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">湘</span>。
- 北逐<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">荤粥</span>，合符<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">釜山</span>，而邑于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">涿鹿之阿</span>。

迁徙往来无常处，以师兵为营卫。官名皆以云命，为<span style="color: #8B0000;">云师</span>。置<span style="color: #8B0000;">左右大监</span>，监于万国。万国和，而鬼神山川封禅与为多焉。获<span style="color: #CD853F;">宝鼎</span>，迎日推筴。举<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">风后</span>、<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">力牧</span>、<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">常先</span>、<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">大鸿</span>以治民。顺天地之纪，幽明之占，死生之说，存亡之难。时播百穀草木，淳化鸟兽蟲蛾，旁罗日月星辰水波土石金玉，劳勤心力耳目，节用水火材物。有土德之瑞，故号<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>。

[3] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>二十五子，其得姓者十四人。

### 后裔与世系

[4] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">轩辕之丘</span>，而娶於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">西陵</span>之女，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">嫘祖</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">嫘祖</span>为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>正妃，生二子，其後皆有天下：

- [4.1] 其一曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">玄嚣</span>，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">青阳</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">青阳</span>降居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">江水</span>；
- [4.2] 其二曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">昌意</span>，降居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">若水</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">昌意</span>娶<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">蜀山氏</span>女，曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">昌仆</span>，生<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高阳</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高阳</span>有圣德焉。

[4.3] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>崩，葬<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">桥山</span>。其孙<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">昌意</span>之子<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高阳</span>立，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝颛顼</span>也。

## <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝颛顼</span>

[5] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝颛顼高阳</span>者，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>之孙而<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">昌意</span>之子也。静渊以有谋，疏通而知事；养材以任地，载时以<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">象</span>天，依鬼神以制义，治气以教化，絜诚以祭祀。

- 北至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">幽陵</span>，
- 南至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">交阯</span>，
- 西至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">流沙</span>，
- 东至于<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">蟠木</span>。

动静之物，大小之神，日月所照，莫不砥属。

[6] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝颛顼</span>生子曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">穷蝉</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">颛顼</span>崩，而<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">玄嚣</span>之孙<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>立，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>。

## <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>

[7] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾高辛</span>者，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>之曾孙也。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>父曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蟜极</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蟜极</span>父曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">玄嚣</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">玄嚣</span>父曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>。自<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">玄嚣</span>与<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">蟜极</span>皆不得在位，至<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>即帝位。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>於<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">颛顼</span>为族子。

[8] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>生而神灵，自言其名。普施利物，不於其身。聪以知远，明以察微。顺天之义，知民之急。仁而威，惠而信，脩身而天下服。

- 取地之财而节用之，
- 抚教万民而利诲之，
- 历日月而迎送之，
- 明鬼神而敬事之。

其色郁郁，其德嶷嶷。其动也时，其服也<span style="color: #8B0000;">士</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>溉执中而遍天下，日月所照，风雨所至，莫不从服。

[9] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>娶<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">陈锋氏</span>女，生<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">放勋</span>。娶<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">娵訾氏</span>女，生<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">挚</span>。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>崩，而<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">挚</span>代立。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝挚</span>立，不善，而弟<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">放勋</span>立，是为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝尧</span>。

## <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝尧</span>

### 钦明文思

[10] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝尧</span>者，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">放勋</span>。其仁如天，其知如神。就之如日，望之如云。富而不骄，贵而不舒。黄收纯衣，彤车乘白马。能明驯德，以亲九族。九族既睦，便章百姓。百姓昭明，合和万国。

### 历法与农时

[11] 乃命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">羲</span>、和，敬顺昊天，数法日月星辰，敬授民时。

- [11.1] 分命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">羲仲</span>，居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">郁夷</span>，曰<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">旸谷</span>。敬道日出，便程东作。<span style="color: #483D8B;">日中</span>，<span style="color: #483D8B;">星鸟</span>，以殷<span style="color: #483D8B;">中春</span>。其民析，鸟兽字微。
- [11.2] 申命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">羲叔</span>，居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">南交</span>。便程南为，敬致。<span style="color: #483D8B;">日永</span>，<span style="color: #483D8B;">星火</span>，以正<span style="color: #483D8B;">中夏</span>。其民因，鸟兽希革。
- [11.3] 申命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">和仲</span>，居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">西土</span>，曰<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">昧谷</span>。敬道日入，便程西成。<span style="color: #483D8B;">夜中</span>，<span style="color: #483D8B;">星虚</span>，以正<span style="color: #483D8B;">中秋</span>。其民夷易，鸟兽毛毨。
- [11.4] 申命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">和叔</span>；居<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">北方</span>，曰<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">幽都</span>。便在伏物。<span style="color: #483D8B;">日短</span>，<span style="color: #483D8B;">星昴</span>，以正<span style="color: #483D8B;">中冬</span>。其民燠，鸟兽氄毛。

[11.5] 岁三百六十六日，以<span style="color: #483D8B;">闰月</span>正四时。信饬百官，众功皆兴。

### 禅让之始

[12]

- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："谁可顺此事？"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">放齐</span>曰："嗣子<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">丹硃</span>开明。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："吁！顽凶，不用。"
- 尧又曰："谁可者？"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">讙兜</span>曰："共工旁聚布功，可用。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："共工善言，其用僻，似恭漫天，不可。"
- 尧又曰："嗟，<span style="color: #8B0000;">四岳</span>，汤汤洪水滔天，浩浩怀山襄陵，下民其忧，有能使治者？"
- 皆曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">鲧</span>可。
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">鲧</span>负命毁族，不可。"
- <span style="color: #8B0000;">岳</span>曰："异哉，试不可用而已。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>於是听岳用<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">鲧</span>。<span style="color: #008B8B;">九岁</span>，功用不成。

[13]

- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："嗟！<span style="color: #8B0000;">四岳</span>：朕在位七十载，汝能庸命，践朕位？"
- <span style="color: #8B0000;">岳</span>应曰："鄙德忝帝位。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："悉举贵戚及疏远隐匿者。"
- 众皆言於<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："有矜在民间，曰<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">虞舜</span>。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："然，朕闻之。其何如？"
- <span style="color: #8B0000;">岳</span>曰："盲者子。父顽，母嚚，弟傲，能和以孝，烝烝治，不至奸。"
- <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰："吾其试哉。"

於是尧妻之二女，观其德於二女。舜饬下二女於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">妫汭</span>，如妇礼。尧善之，乃使<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>慎<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">和</span><span style="color: #4682B4;">五典</span>，<span style="color: #4682B4;">五典</span>能从。乃遍入百官，百官时序。宾於四门，四门穆穆，诸侯远方宾客皆敬。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>使<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>入山林川泽，暴风雷雨，舜行不迷。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>以为圣，召<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>曰："女谋事至而言可绩，<span style="color: #008B8B;">三年</span>矣。女登帝位。"<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>让於德不怿。<span style="color: #008B8B;">正月上日</span>，舜受终於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">文祖</span>。<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">文祖</span>者，尧大祖也。

## <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝舜</span>

### 摄政与礼法

[14] 於是<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝尧</span>老，命<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>摄行<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>之政，以观天命。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>乃在<span style="color: #CD853F;">璿玑玉衡</span>，以齐<span style="color: #483D8B;">七政</span>。遂类于上帝，禋于六宗，望于山川，辩于群神。揖<span style="color: #CD853F;">五瑞</span>，择吉月日，见<span style="color: #8B0000;">四岳</span>诸牧，班瑞。

[14.1] <span style="color: #008B8B;">岁二月</span>，东巡狩，至於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">岱宗</span>，祡，望秩於山川。遂见东方君长，合时月正日，同律度量衡，脩<span style="color: #4682B4;">五礼</span>五玉三帛二生一死为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">挚</span>，如<span style="color: #CD853F;">五器</span>，卒乃复。<span style="color: #008B8B;">五月</span>，南巡狩；<span style="color: #008B8B;">八月</span>，西巡狩；<span style="color: #008B8B;">十一月</span>，北巡狩：皆如初。归，至于祖祢庙，用特牛礼。

[14.2] <span style="color: #008B8B;">五岁</span>一巡狩，群后四朝。遍告以言，明试以功，车服以庸。肇十有二州，决川。象以<span style="color: #4682B4;">典刑</span>，<span style="color: #4682B4;">流宥五刑</span>，鞭作<span style="color: #4682B4;">官刑</span>，扑作<span style="color: #4682B4;">教刑</span>，金作<span style="color: #4682B4;">赎刑</span>。眚灾过，赦；怙终贼，刑。钦哉，钦哉，惟刑之静哉！

### 惩办四罪

[15] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">讙兜</span>进言共工，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>曰不可而试之<span style="color: #8B0000;">工师</span>，共工果淫辟。<span style="color: #8B0000;">四岳</span>举<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">鲧</span>治鸿水，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>以为不可，<span style="color: #8B0000;">岳</span>彊请试之，试之而无功，故百姓不便。<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">三苗</span>在<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">江淮</span>、<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">荆州</span>数为乱。於是<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>归而言於帝，

- [15.1] 请流共工於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">幽陵</span>，以变<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">北狄</span>；
- [15.2] 放驩兜於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">崇山</span>，以变<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">南蛮</span>；
- [15.3] 迁<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">三苗</span>於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">三危</span>，以变<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">西戎</span>；

- [24.9] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">禹</span>之功为大，披九山，通九泽，决九河，定九州，各以其职来贡，不失厥宜。方五千里，至于荒服。南抚<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">交阯</span>、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">北发</span>，<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">西戎</span>、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">析枝</span>、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">渠廋</span>、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">氐</span>、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">羌</span>，北<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">山戎</span>、发、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">息慎</span>，东长、<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">鸟夷</span>，四<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">海</span>之内咸戴<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝舜</span>之功。於是<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">禹</span>乃兴九招之乐，致异物，凤皇来翔。天下明德皆自<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">虞帝</span>始。

[25] 舜<span style="color: #008B8B;">年二十</span>以孝闻，<span style="color: #008B8B;">年三十</span><span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>举之，<span style="color: #008B8B;">年五十</span>摄行<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>事，<span style="color: #008B8B;">年五十八</span><span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>崩，<span style="color: #008B8B;">年六十一</span>代尧践帝位。践帝位<span style="color: #008B8B;">三十九年</span>，南巡狩，崩於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">苍梧之野</span>。葬於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">江南九疑</span>，是为<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">零陵</span>。

[25.1] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>之践帝位，载<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>旗，往朝父<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">瞽叟</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">夔</span><span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">夔</span>唯谨，如子道。封弟<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">象</span>为诸侯。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>子<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">商均</span>亦不肖，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>乃豫荐<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">禹</span>於天。<span style="color: #008B8B;">十七年</span>而崩。<span style="color: #008B8B;">三年</span>丧毕，禹亦乃让<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>子，如<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>让<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>子。诸侯归之，然後禹践<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>位。

[25.2] 尧子<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">丹硃</span>，<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>子<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">商均</span>，皆有疆土，以奉先祀。服其服，礼乐如之。以客见<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>，<span style="color: #8B0000;">天子</span>弗臣，示不敢专也。

## 总结：五帝世系

[26] 自<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>至舜、禹，皆同姓而异其国号，以章明德。

- [26.1] 故<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">有熊</span>，
- [26.2] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝颛顼</span>为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高阳</span>，
- [26.3] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝喾</span>为<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">高辛</span>，
- [26.4] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝尧</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">陶唐</span>，
- [26.5] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝舜</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">有虞</span>。
- [26.6] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">帝禹</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">夏后</span>而别氏，姓姒氏。
- [26.7] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">契</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">商</span>，姓子氏。
- [26.8] <span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">弃</span>为<span style="color: #9370DB; background-color: #F0E6FF;">周</span>，姓姬氏。

## 太史公曰

[27] 太史公曰：学者多称五帝，尚矣。然《尚书》独载<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">尧</span>以来；而百家言<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>，其文不雅驯，荐绅先生难言之。<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">孔子</span>所传<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">宰予</span>问五帝德及帝系姓，儒者或不传。余尝西至<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">空桐</span>，北过<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">涿鹿</span>，东渐於<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">海</span>，南浮<span style="color: #B8860B; text-decoration: underline;">江淮</span>矣，至长老皆各往往称<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">黄帝</span>、尧、<span style="color: #8B4513; text-decoration: underline;">舜</span>之处，风教固殊焉，总之不离古文者近是。予观《春秋》、《国语》，其<span style="color: #2F4F4F;">发</span>明五帝德、帝系姓章矣，顾弟弗深考，其所表见皆不虚。书缺有间矣，其轶乃时时见於他说。非好学深思，心知其意，固难为浅见寡闻道也。余并论次，择其言尤雅者，故著为本纪书首。
